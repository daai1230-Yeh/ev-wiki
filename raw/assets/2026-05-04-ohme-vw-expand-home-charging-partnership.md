# Ohme and VW expand home charging partnership

**Author**: Sebastian Schaal | **Date**: 2026-04-30

---

Drivers of select electric models from six Volkswagen Group brands can now charge more easily, intelligently, and affordably at home thanks to a new integration by Ohme and Volkswagen Group Info Services AG.

## What's New

Ohme — a British EV home charging solutions provider — has extended its VW Group partnership from the UK/Ireland to the rest of Europe. The integration uses an official API to pair the car with Ohme home chargers and read the vehicle's state of charge in near real time.

**Covered brands**: VW, VW Commercial Vehicles, Škoda, SEAT, Cupra, and Audi (MEB platform models)

## How It Works

Ohme's smart chargers (e.g. Wallbox Home Pro, Home Pro MID) automatically shift charging sessions to lower-price electricity periods, reducing costs without manual scheduling. With a flexible electricity tariff, significant annual savings are possible.

The integration displays vehicle state of charge directly in the Ohme app — removing guesswork and improving clarity over energy use and costs.

## Scale

Over 100,000 Volkswagen Group customers across Europe already use Ohme for home charging. In the UK, Ohme is already the official home charging partner for Mercedes-Benz, Hyundai, Smart, Xpeng, and Genesis.
