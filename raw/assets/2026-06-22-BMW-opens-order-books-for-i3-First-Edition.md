# BMW opens order books for i3 First Edition

**Author**: Cora Werwitzke | **Date**: 2026-06-19

---

The First Edition of BMW's new electric i3 mid-size sedan is now available for order—starting at £57,905—ahead of its official autumn 2026 market launch. BMW advanced the order start in response to high demand.

With the BMW i3 50 xDrive First Edition, the fully electric BMW 3 Series is now available for order. BMW has also revealed the price for the later base version, the i3 50 xDrive, which will start at £53,005.

The i3 50 xDrive features a 345 kW all-wheel-drive system and a 108 kWh battery. The i3 50 xDrive First Edition achieves a WLTP range of up to 906 kilometres, while the standard i3 50 xDrive reaches up to 912 kilometres.

The electric saloon is based on an 800-volt architecture and supports DC charging at up to 400 kW. The battery can be charged from 10 to 80 per cent in 21 minutes. The new BMW i3 also supports bidirectional charging functions.

The new BMW i3 is powered by the sixth generation of BMW's electric drivetrain technology and accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in 4.7 seconds. The dual-motor all-wheel-drive system delivers a maximum torque output of 645 Nm.

The First Edition stands out through an enhanced equipment package, including the M Sport Package, the 'BMW Iconic Glow' exterior package, and a 3D Head-Up Display as standard. Additional standard equipment includes electrically adjustable multifunction front seats, automatic tailgate, three-zone automatic climate control, Harman Kardon sound system and 22 kW AC charging capability.

Production of the i3 is set to begin in August at BMW's Munich headquarters, with deliveries expected to start in late summer.
