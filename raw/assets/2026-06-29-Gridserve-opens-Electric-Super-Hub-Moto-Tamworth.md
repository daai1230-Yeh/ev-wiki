# Gridserve opens Electric Super Hub at Moto Tamworth

**Author**: Carla Westerheide | **Date**: 2026-06-29

---

**Gridserve** has opened a new **Electric Super Hub** at **Moto Tamworth** (West Midlands, UK) with **32 ultra-rapid charging bays**.

## Facility Layout

- **24 bays** for passenger cars, including 3 accessible bays; up to **360 kW** per charger
- **8 bays** for large vehicles (extended-wheelbase vans, electric coaches, HGVs, cars+trailers); drive-through layout with **350 kW** chargers on both sides
- Compatible vehicles can add **100 miles in under 10 minutes**
- Operates on **net-zero energy**; supports contactless payment

## Infrastructure

- **14 MVA primary substation** — enough to power a large town
- New **33 kV cable** infrastructure installed to connect to the grid
- Partners: Gridserve, Moto Hospitality, National Grid Electricity Distribution, Last Mile (IDNO)
- UK government's **Green Recovery Fund** funded cable infrastructure; substation jointly funded by Gridserve and Moto

## Policy Context

Gridserve CEO Daniel Kunkel credited the UK's **ZEV mandate** for providing the policy certainty needed to unlock this level of private sector investment.
