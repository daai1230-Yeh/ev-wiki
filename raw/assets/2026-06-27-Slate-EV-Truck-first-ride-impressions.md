# Is The $25K Slate Truck Just Too Basic? I Rode In One To Find Out.

**Author**: InsideEVs Staff | **Date**: 2026-06-27

---

The **Slate Truck** is priced at **$24,950**, making it both the cheapest new electric vehicle and lowest-priced pickup in the US. Orders opened June 24 (with a catch). An InsideEVs editor took a ride to assess whether the deliberately stripped-down approach is a feature or a flaw.

## Key Details

- Price: **$24,950** (sub-$30K)
- Minimalist/basic design philosophy — customers add what they need
- Competes with: Chevy Bolt, Nissan Leaf at the price point; pickup-truck buyers at the segment level

## The "Catch"

Orders opened June 24, but the vehicle is deliberately basic — no frills, minimal features — targeting buyers who want simple, affordable EV utility.
