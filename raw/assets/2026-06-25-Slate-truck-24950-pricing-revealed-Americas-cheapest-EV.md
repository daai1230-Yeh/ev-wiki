# Slate's $24,950 EV Truck Will Test What America Really Wants

**Author**: Tim Levin | **Date**: June 24, 2026

---

Jeff Bezos-backed Slate Auto has finally revealed the official price of its long-awaited electric truck. The bare-bones pickup will start at **$24,950** before destination fees, making it America's most affordable EV and also the country's cheapest truck.

Key details:
- **Price**: $24,950 (before destination fees)
- **Battery**: New lithium-iron-phosphate (LFP) battery, made by **Gotion in Illinois**
- **Range**: Manufacturer-estimated 205 miles
- **Accessories**: Over 200 accessories at launch, more than 80% costing under $500
- The Slate will be the country's cheapest new electric vehicle and its lowest-priced pickup

The vehicle features crank-up windows, modest electric range, and virtually limitless customization options. Tim Levin rode in the Slate Truck at a press event in Los Angeles. Orders for Slate's sub-$30K bare-bones EV truck opened on June 24, 2026.
