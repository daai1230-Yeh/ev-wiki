# Electric powerhouse: Mercedes-AMG unveils GT 4-Door Coupé

**Author**: Cora Werwitzke | **Date**: 2026-05-20

---

Mercedes-AMG has unveiled the all-electric GT 4-Door Coupé with up to 860 kW of output and a peak charging capacity of more than 600 kW. Orders for the high-performance model will open in the coming days, initially with the GT 63 and GT 55 powertrain variants.

The all-electric GT 4-Door Coupé is the first model built on the Mercedes-AMG vehicle platform AMG.EA and is powered by three axial flux motors, which combine to deliver up to 860 kW of peak power. Mercedes emphasises that the model embodies 'Affalterbach's pioneering spirit with numerous world firsts.'

The four-door GT Coupé introduces several innovations. Mercedes-AMG is using axial flux motors in a production model for the first time and combines them with an 800-volt battery derived from Formula 1 technology. Its directly cooled cylindrical cells are designed to deliver fast, repeatable and sustained power output.

Two powertrain versions will be available when orders open: the 63 and 55 versions. Pricing is based on comparable predecessor vehicles, currently priced at around €179,000 and €158,800, respectively. The vehicle is expected to accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in just 2.1 seconds, and from 0 to 200 km/h in 6.4 seconds, with a top speed of 300 km/h.

The AMG High Performance Electric Battery (AMG HP.EB) incorporates experience from Formula 1 and the development of the Mercedes-AMG ONE hypercar. The battery is designed to deliver AMG performance while providing a range appropriate for its price class — currently specified at 600 to 700 kilometres, with potential for "well over 700 kilometres" WLTP range in the future.

Key battery innovations include 2,660 cylindrical cells with custom-developed NCMA chemistry, innovative direct oil-based cooling for each individual cell, and an 800-volt architecture. The peak charging power exceeds 600 kW, enabling a 10–80% SoC charge in just 11 minutes, or enough range for ~460 km in ten minutes.

**Mercedes-AMG GT 55 4-Door Coupé Key Specs:**
- Three axial flux motors, 600 kW (816 hp) max power
- AWD, 800V battery, 106 kWh (net)
- 0–100 km/h: 2.8 sec, Top speed: 300 km/h
- DC charging: 600 kW peak, 10–80% in 11 min
- WLTP range: 597–700 km
- Dimensions: 5,094 / 1,959 / 1,411 mm, Cd: 0.22
