Benefits of LFP manufacturing in India 

LFP is the Preferred Cathode Chemistry

• Due to the tropical climate and cost efficiency, LFP is the most preferred Cathode 

Chemistry in India

Raw Material Availability for LFP in India

• India enjoys self-sufficiency in the production of iron ore, along with a sufficient supply of 

phosphorous acid.

• Therefore, compared to other battery chemistries, LFP batteries in India have lower risk of 

supply chain

Strong Demand Scenario

• Total annual demand for LFP cathode material by 2030 in India will be 226 thousand 
tonnes in base case scenario and 385 thousand tonnes in promising case scenario

• (source: PwC market survey)

Proposed LIB Raw Material Hub at Southern State of Tamil Nadu 

Himadri wants to set up a Raw Material Hub for LIB for manufacturing Anode and 

Cathode material for 100 GWh equivalent capacity .

Anode Material & Cathode Material constitute ~65% of the cost of LIB cell and

~55% of the constituents of a LIB cell by weight .

Glimpse of the Project Location & Benefits :
Proposed location at only 50 kms from the Chennai sea port. We have provisionally booked 150 acres of
land parcel for the project .
Expected Benefits from the project :

 Subsidy on turnover totaling significant percentage of capital expenditure calculated on

NPV basis.

 Reduced Land price
 Electricity supply at around 6 US Cents per KWh , which is almost 60% of the normal rate
 Interest subvention on term loan
 Workmen Training Subsidy
 Stamp Duty exemption on purchase / lease of land
