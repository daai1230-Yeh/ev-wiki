# Electra extends roaming network to 800,000 charge points

**Author**: Daniel Bönnighausen | **Date**: 2026-06-29

---

French charging operator **Electra** has expanded its app into a **Europe-wide roaming platform**, giving users access to ~**800,000 public charging points** across Europe via a single app — transitioning from a pure CPO to an **eMobility Service Provider (eMSP)**.

## Network Access

- Electra's own fast-charging hubs
- ChargeLeague partners (Ionity, Fastned, Atlante)
- Third-party networks across Europe (~800K total charge points)

## German Pricing Snapshot

| Provider | Price |
|---|---|
| Electra own (ad-hoc) | €0.69/kWh |
| Electra+ Smart (sub) | from €0.34/kWh |
| Electra+ Essential (sub) | from €0.44/kWh |
| Mer (roaming) | €0.71/kWh |
| Shell/EnBW (roaming) | €0.79/kWh |
| Aral (roaming) | €0.96/kWh |

## New Features

- **Smart navigation algorithm**: recommends charging points by power, availability, reliability, location, and price
- **RFID charging card**: €4.99 one-time fee (free for subscribers)
- **Autocharge**: plug-in auto-authentication (requires vehicle with fixed VID)
- **Hanover flagship hub** opens July 1: 14 charging points, 7 stations, up to 400 kW each

## Scale

Electra currently operates ~**700 charging hubs** with ~**4,000 charging points** in 10 countries. Target: **15,000 fast-charging points by 2030**.
