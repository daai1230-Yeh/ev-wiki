# Germany: VWCV cuts prices for electric models by up to €10,000

**Author**: Sebastian Schaal | **Date**: 2026-04-21

---

Volkswagen Commercial Vehicles (VWCV) is offering a special discount of up to €10,000 on its electric models in Germany until May 29. Depending on the model, discounts range from €5,800 to the aforementioned €10,000 — available not just for private customers.

The discounts apply to vehicles registered for the first time by 31 December 2026.

## Discount Breakdown by Model

| Model | Discount | Customer |
|-------|----------|----------|
| Volkswagen ID. Buzz | up to **€6,000** | Private |
| Volkswagen e-Caravelle | up to **€6,700** | Private |
| ID. Buzz Cargo | up to **€6,600** | Business |
| e-Transporter Kombi (T7) | up to **€5,800** | All |
| e-Transporter panel van / flatbed (T7) | up to **€10,000** | All |

Private buyers may additionally be eligible for the federal government's EV subsidy, which can be combined with the VW discount (subject to income bracket eligibility).

## Key Conditions

- Applies to "available new vehicles without registration from the manufacturer's stock" — not individually configured vehicles.
- Choice of payment method (cash, leasing, financing) does not affect eligibility.
- Excludes bulk buyers and special customers.
- As the Volkswagen ID. Buzz has recently switched from the 2026 to the 2027 model year in ordering systems, the campaign is likely aimed at clearing remaining 2026 inventory.

## Background

The e-Transporter (T7) is produced by Ford Otosan in Turkey, based on the Ford E-Transit Custom, and benefits from Ford's recent battery upgrade. The ID. Buzz, built at VW's Hanover plant on the MEB platform, offers a larger battery and greater range than the e-Transporter in its short-wheelbase version.
