# UK ultra-fast charging hub sets the benchmark in Europe

**Author**: Advertising Partner | **Date**: 22.04.2026 - 13:00

---

CASE STUDY — UK ultra-fast charging hub exceeds 60,000 annual charging sessions, leading the way for fast charging in Europe.

In 2025, a major European charging hub located in the United Kingdom faced a critical challenge: rapidly growing EV traffic was putting increasing pressure on its existing charging infrastructure. The operator needed a solution capable of delivering consistent ultra fast charging power, minimizing congestion, and supporting future expansion.

To address these needs, the operator deployed Power Electronics' NB Station 1.44 MW ultra fast charging system paired with 8 Slim double dispensers, creating a robust configuration of 16 simultaneous charging points. This setup was specifically engineered to improve user experience, reduce waiting times, and maintain operational stability even during periods of peak demand.
