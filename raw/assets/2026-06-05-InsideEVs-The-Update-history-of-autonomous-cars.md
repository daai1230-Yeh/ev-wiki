**The Self-Driving Car Story Started Earlier Than You Think**
Decades before Waymo and Tesla, a Mercedes van used cameras and computers to drive itself down the autobahn — back in 1995, sometimes at autobahn speeds.

**Old Waymo Batteries Will Get A Second Life As Stationary Energy Storage**
Waymo struck a deal with B2U Storage Solutions to ensure its old robotaxi batteries live on as stationary storage.
