# Mahle chargeBIG unveils new AC charging station with four charging points

**Author**: Florian Treiss | **Date**: 18.06.2026

---

At Power2Drive (23–25 June, Munich), Mahle chargeBIG is presenting the **smallBOX Public** — a charging terminal supplying up to four EVs simultaneously at 22 kW AC each. Developed in cooperation with Langmatz Energy.

The integrated design requires no additional charging pillars, saving space in parking structures. Includes an integrated payment terminal (RFID card or credit card). The outdoor housing is optimised for Mahle chargeBIG's dynamic, grid-relieving AC charging technology.

Also unveiled: two new financing models (with BNP Paribas Leasing Solutions) — classic leasing (predictable monthly payments) and hire purchase (ownership at end of term). No high upfront investment needed.

Also showcased at Power2Drive (Hall C6, Stand 320):
- **smallBOX Portable**: mobile solution for construction sites/events/temporary use — 4 charging points, up to 22 kW each, no permanent installation
- New **cooling module** for DC fast chargers to dissipate cable heat under extreme climatic conditions
