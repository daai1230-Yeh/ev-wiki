# Porsche unveils Cayenne Coupé Electric

**Author**: Florian Treiss | **Date**: 2026-04-24

---

Porsche celebrated the world premiere of the new Cayenne Coupé Electric at Auto China in Beijing. The vehicle is now available for order and comes in all three drivetrain variants already known from the Cayenne Electric E-SUV. The price list in Germany starts at €109,000.

Porsche introduced the electric version of the standard Cayenne in November. Initially, the launch included the base model without any additional designation and a Turbo variant, followed by the mid-range Cayenne S Electric in March. Series production of the vehicles began in February in Bratislava, Slovakia, on the same production line as the familiar petrol and hybrid Cayenne models.

## Three Variants

The lineup includes three drivetrain variants:

- **Cayenne Coupé Electric** (base): 300 kW (408 hp), 0–62 mph in 4.8 seconds
- **Cayenne S Coupé Electric**: 400 kW normally, 490 kW with overboost (667 hp), 0–62 mph in 3.8 seconds
- **Cayenne Turbo Coupé Electric**: 630 kW, up to 850 kW (1,156 hp) with boost, 0–62 mph in 2.5 seconds

## Range and Charging

The combined WLTP range reaches up to 669 kilometres. The top-spec Turbo variant uses an 800-volt architecture with a 113 kWh battery pack and DC fast-charging at up to 400 kW, with a 10-to-80 percent charge taking under 16 minutes.

The Coupé features a sloping roofline that improves aerodynamic drag to a coefficient of 0.23 (compared to 0.25 for the SUV), inspired by the iconic 911 silhouette.

## Availability

Deliveries are set for late summer 2026. Orders are open in Germany and other markets.
