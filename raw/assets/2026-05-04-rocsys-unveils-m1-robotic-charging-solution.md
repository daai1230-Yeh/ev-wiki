# Rocsys unveils next version of its robotic charging solution

**Author**: Cora Werwitzke | **Date**: 2026-04-30

---

Rocsys has unveiled its new M1 robotic charging solution, enabling automated charging of multiple vehicles simultaneously for robotaxi fleets. The company also raised $13 million in new funding.

## Rocsys M1 — Key Features

- **Modular architecture**: One system serves multiple vehicles across up to 10 bays simultaneously
- **Installation options**: Floor, wall, or ceiling mounting
- **Performance**: 99.9%+ plug-in success rate in live environments
- **Technology**: AI-powered computer vision + motion intelligence for safe, context-aware operation
- **Compatibility**: All types of EVs, chargers, and plug types
- **Status**: Currently in pilot phase; large-scale deployment planned for 2027
- **Demo**: ACT Expo (4–6 May) in Las Vegas

The system handles cable insertion and removal autonomously, making fleet charging scalable without human intervention. It's designed for autonomous electric vehicle depot operations.

## Funding

- **$13 million Series A extension** led by Capricorn Partners
- Other investors: Scania Invest, Forward.One, SEB Greentech Venture Capital, Graduate Ventures
- **Total funding to date**: $56 million

CEO Crijn Bouman noted a signed "major robotaxi deal" (undisclosed customer). The platform is designed to extend beyond charging to automated interior cleaning and inspection.
