# I Drove The 2027 Volvo EX60 And Think It's The Best Volvo Ever. All Because It's Electric.

**Author**: Mack Hogan | **Date**: 2026-05-20 | **Source**: InsideEVs

---

Volvo's focus on minimalist luxury has always been a natural fit with EVs. With the EX60, the company finally sticks the landing. Over five hours of back-country and city driving around Barcelona, the Volvo EX60 was exceptionally smooth, nearly silent, and utterly charming.

## Key Specs

| Spec | P6 (RWD) | P10 (AWD) |
|------|----------|-----------|
| Base price | $59,795 | $62,145 |
| Range (EPA) | 307 miles | 322 miles |
| Battery | 83 kWh (80 usable) | 95 kWh |
| Power | 369 hp | 503 hp |
| 0–60 mph | 5.7 sec | 4.4 sec |
| DC charge (max) | 320 kW | 370 kW |
| 10–80% charge time | 16 min | 16 min |
| AC charging | 19.2 kW | 19.2 kW |
| Cargo (behind 2nd row) | 20.4 cu ft | – |

A top-spec all-wheel-drive P12 arrives later in 2026, offering up to **400 miles of EPA range** from a 117 kWh pack and 670 hp for 3.8-second 0–60.

## Interior

- Clean, minimalist cabin with a landscape touchscreen and head-up display
- Physical buttons for volume, track skip, and key functions (meets EU requirements)
- Standard **Bowers & Wilkins 28-speaker Dolby Atmos** system on Ultra trims — reviewer's highlight
- Headrest speakers can isolate audio to one passenger — useful for sleeping passengers
- No more window-switch design issues from the EX90

## Software & Tech

- Powered by **Google Android Automotive** with Google Built-In
- **Conversational Gemini AI navigation**: natural language queries like "find me a 350 kW charger near a dog-friendly park along my route"
- Smooth, intuitive UI with no noticeable bugs in 5 hours of testing

## Driving Impression

"Everything that is great about this car is great because it's electric." — Volvo comms boss Thomas McIntyre Schultz

The EX60 feels polished, quiet, and confident. The silent drivetrain enhances the stereo, the comfort, and the cabin refinement. Reviewer Mack Hogan: "I just couldn't get over how polished the whole thing felt... the EX60 finally feels like a true Volvo, through and through."

## Verdict

Not yet declared best-in-class (iX3 and Rivian R2 untested), but the EX60 delivers what Volvo buyers want: a serene, competent, luxurious companion. In the luxury midsize crossover segment, price parity with gas cars has arrived — and the EV is simply the better car.
