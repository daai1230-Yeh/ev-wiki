# Sunsundegui's trademarks, patents and after-sales business go to auction

**Author**: Editorial staff | **Date**: 2026-06-29

---

The insolvency administrators of Spanish coach bodybuilder **Sunsundegui** have launched a **public auction for the company's trademarks, patents and technical support business**. The company has ceased operations in 2025.

The sale covers its brand identity, industrial property and technical support activities, with the process managed by **specialist asset sales company Surus through its Escrapalia online platform**.

## Assets offered in three lots

The assets are being offered in three separate lots:

- **Lot 1 – Trademarks and distinctive signs:** the Sunsundegui brand, logos and trade names, with established recognition in Spain and international markets, plus corporate internet domains.
- **Lot 2 – Industrial designs, bus models, engineering and patents:** bus models, construction drawings, technical documentation, approvals and technological developments for the SC7, SC5 and SB3 models. Includes editable CAD, 3D and DWG engineering drawings, registered industrial designs, and patents related to structural anchoring systems and rollover protection (UNECE R66 compliant).
- **Lot 3 – Technical Assistance Service (SAT) and strategic spare parts:** the after-sales business, workshop manuals, wiring diagrams, Electronic Parts Catalogue (EPC), inventories of original spare parts, and agreements with authorized service workshops.

## Timeline and bidders

The deadline for bids covering the trademarks and patents is **15 September 2026**. Interested parties may access a virtual data room containing technical, commercial and employment documentation before submitting bids.

According to Surus, the assets may be of interest to bus and coach manufacturers, electric mobility companies and after-sales service providers seeking to expand through an established brand, engineering portfolio and existing customer base.
