# World premiere: VW ID. Polo GTI – a compact electric sports car with major impact?

**作者**：Daniel Bönnighausen　｜　**日期**：2026-05-15

---

Volkswagen is bringing the GTI into the electric era while drawing heavily on the badge's heritage. A red stripe, tartan trim and GTI mode meet 166 kW, an adaptive chassis and a front-axle differential lock. Pre-sales for the ID. Polo GTI will open in autumn at prices starting just below €39,000.

## Specifications

- **Powertrain**: APP290 electric motor, 166 kW / 290 Nm, front-wheel drive
- **Top speed**: 175 kph (15 kph higher than standard ID. Polo)
- **Battery**: 52 kWh NMC (Cell-to-Pack technology, unified cell format)
- **Range**: Up to 424 km WLTP
- **AC charging**: 11 kW onboard charger, full charge in ~5.5 hours
- **DC charging**: Up to 105 kW, 10-80% in ~24 minutes
- **Platform**: MEB+ (latest evolution)
- **Dimensions**: ~4.10 m long, ~1.82 m wide, 2.6 m wheelbase (7 mm longer than standard)
- **Starting price**: Just under €39,000

## Key features

- Standard front-axle differential lock (same as Golf GTI)
- DCC adaptive damper control on both axles
- Dedicated 'GTI' driving profile (alongside Eco, Comfort, Sport, Individual)
- GTI mode activates Launch Control and seatbelt pretensioners
- Classic GTI design cues: red stripe, tartan-pattern seats, red 12 o'clock steering wheel marker

## Context

The ID. Polo GTI is Volkswagen's first fully battery-electric model bearing the GTI badge, 50 years after the original GTI debuted. The 37 kWh LFP battery available in the base ID. Polo is not offered on the GTI. The model replaces the ICE Polo GTI which was discontinued in 2026.
