# Philippines: Nissan to launch China-made N7 as the 'Primera EV'

**Author**: Sagar Parikh | **Date**: 2026-06-15

---

Nissan has announced plans to launch the N7 electric mid-size saloon as the 'Primera EV' in the Philippines. The country is set to become the first overseas market for the China-made EV.

Nissan held the local premiere of the Primera EV at the tenth Philippine International Motor Show (PIMS), reviving the Primera nameplate last used for an ICE model in the 2000s. The company plans to launch the model in Q4 2026, having already registered it with the Philippine Department of Energy.

The Philippines is not Nissan's only target market outside China. The company filed trademark applications for 'Primera' in Oman, Bahrain and Malaysia, showcased the N7 at the Japan Mobility Show in October 2025, and in April 2026 announced that its Dongfeng joint venture would export the N7 to Latin American and ASEAN markets.

The Chinese-spec N7 measures 4,930 mm in length with a 2.9 m wheelbase. Two FWD powertrain options are available: 160 kW with a 58 kWh LFP battery (up to 540 km CLTC), and 200 kW with a 73 kWh LFP battery (up to 635 km CLTC).
