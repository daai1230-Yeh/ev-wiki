# Leadership change at Spirii as founder Harritshøj steps down

**Author**: Sebastian Schaal | **Date**: 23.04.2026 - 13:30

---

Danish charging platform Spirii appoints Mathias Wiecher as its new CEO. He succeeds co-founder Tore Harritshøj, who has led the company since its inception and will now step down.

Mathias Wiecher (45) brings more than 12 years of international experience in e-mobility, energy, and transport. Most recently, he served as General Manager for Energy and Charging Infrastructure at Einride since September 2024. Additionally, Wiecher previously sat on the supervisory boards of Virta and Hubject, giving him in-depth knowledge of the charging industry. He also spent many years working at E.ON.
