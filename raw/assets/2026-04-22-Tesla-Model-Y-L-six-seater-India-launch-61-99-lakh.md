# Tesla launches six-seater Model Y L in India at ₹61.99 lakh

**Author**: ETAuto | **Date**: 2026-04-22

---

Tesla has launched the Model Y L in India at ₹61.99 lakh, adding a long-wheelbase variant with a six-seat layout to its India lineup — the first Model Y in India with this configuration.

**Key specs:**
- Price: ₹61.99 lakh (ex-showroom)
- Seating: 6 seats (2+2+2 configuration)
- Wheelbase: 3,040mm (extended)
- Range: 681 km
- Powertrain: Dual-motor AWD
- Monthly financing options from ₹49,000

**Deliveries:** Scheduled to begin in June 2026 across India.

**Customer experience:** The Model Y L will be available for test drives from 23 April 2026 at Tesla locations in BKC (Mumbai), Aerocity (Delhi), and Orchid Business Park (Gurugram).

**Market positioning:** The Model Y L targets the family SUV segment in India, competing with luxury three-row SUVs. The six-seat configuration with the extended wheelbase is designed to offer more cabin space than the standard Model Y, which has a five-seat layout.

The launch comes as Tesla continues its expansion in the Indian market, positioning the longer-range, larger Model Y L as a premium family offering alongside the standard Model Y.
