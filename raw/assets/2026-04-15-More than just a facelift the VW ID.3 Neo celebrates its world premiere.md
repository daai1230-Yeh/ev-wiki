# More than just a facelift: the VW ID.3 Neo celebrates its world premiere

**Author**: Daniel Bönnighausen | **Date**: 15.04.2026 - 12:01

---

Volkswagen has officially unveiled the comprehensively updated ID.3, now carrying the 'Neo' suffix. The compact EV is the first of six new electric models set to launch this year – with revisions that go well beyond a typical facelift. We had the opportunity to experience the ID.3 Neo firsthand and took a closer look at the details.

Just days ago, Volkswagen invited media representatives to Hamburg, where the carmaker struck an unusually self-critical tone. Martin Sander acknowledged that the brand had lost focus on what matters most: the customer. Under the banner 'True Volkswagen', the company is now setting a new course – electric, of course.
