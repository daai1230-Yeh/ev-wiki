# The Volkswagen ID. Buzz Returns For 2027 With New Trims And One Big Fix Owners Wanted

**Author**: Suvrat Kothari | **Date**: May 14, 2026

---

Volkswagen said the ID. Buzz will return for model year 2027 with a bunch of small upgrades inside and out.

After skipping the current model year, the ID. Buzz will join Volkswagen's model year 2027 lineup. It now gets a new dedicated trim for camping, with a bunch of cool features straight from the factory. Most importantly: **true one-pedal driving** has finally been added — a big miss on MY2025 vehicles.

The Volkswagen ID. Buzz will rejoin the U.S. lineup with two new trims, updated software, and revised color and wheel options. The electric minivan skipped MY2026 due to low demand, going from MY2025 straight to MY2027.

**New Tourer 4Motion trim (camping-focused):** Includes a fold-out mattress and platform, window blinds, ventilation panels, a folding table and chair set, and an "Overnight Mode" that uses the high-voltage battery to run climate control while the vehicle is switched off.

**Software:** The ID. Buzz now gets ID.S 6 software with improved infotainment UX — revised navigation layout, new vehicle settings page, and an updated App Store supporting third-party apps like YouTube and Spotify.

**Paint:** Dual-tone paint is now standard (previously optional). Only dual-tone schemes are available, with a new Candy White over Cherry Red option and available white wheels inspired by the Type 2 microbus.

**Trim lineup:** Revised to four models: Pro S RWD, Pro S 4Motion (new), Tourer 4Motion (new), and Pro S Plus 4Motion.

Pricing hasn't been announced; the MY2025 started at $60,000 before destination. A price drop into the low $50,000s could significantly boost appeal.
