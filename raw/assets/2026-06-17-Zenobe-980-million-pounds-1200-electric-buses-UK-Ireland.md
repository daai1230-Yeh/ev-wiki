# Zenobē raises £980 million to finance 1,200 additional electric buses in the UK and Ireland

**Author**: Editorial staff | **Date**: 2026-06-17

---

**Zenobē** has secured **£980 million** in new financing to deploy **1,200+ electric buses** and associated charging infrastructure across the UK and Ireland — the company's third funding round for its UK/Ireland EV debt platform.

## Key Numbers

- **£980M** new financing (third round)
- Total capital raised since 2017: **£3.2 billion**
- Target: **5,000 electric buses by 2028** across UK and Europe combined
- **22 banks and institutional investors** now in the platform (10 new this round)

## Context

- Owned by **KKR** and **Infracapital** (infrastructure investors)
- Model: full-service fleet electrification — vehicle financing + charging infrastructure + energy management
- Also announced: **€325M European EV fleet facility** (2025), **€120M leasing framework with Vectalia** (Spain)

## Business Case

Diesel fuel ≈ **40% of vehicle lifetime cost** vs. ~**20% for electricity** in EVs — making electrification financially attractive for operators beyond just regulatory compliance.

**Steven Meersman (Founder)**: "Operators today are acutely aware of the implications that diesel reliance has on their bottom-line. We're here to support operators that want to make a switch that makes economic and environmental sense at meaningful scale."
