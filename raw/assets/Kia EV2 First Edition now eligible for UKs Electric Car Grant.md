# Kia EV2 'First Edition' now eligible for UK's Electric Car Grant

**Author**: Ciaran Daly | **Date**: 15.04.2026 - 17:00

---

Kia UK has announced that the EV2 'First Edition' will join the EV3, EV4, and PV5 Passenger in being eligible for the £1,500 Electric Car Grant (ECG). It replaces the Kia EV2 'Reservation Saving' previously available for the EV2 'First Edition'.

In real terms, then, the price tag hasn't changed for consumers since the car's initial UK launch. The 'Kia EV2 Reservation Saving' was available to customers who reserved the 'First Edition' from April 1, 2026, and bringing the on-the-road price down to £26,995 (from £28,495). Now, instead of the £1,500 discount coming from Kia, it's coming from the UK government's ECG fund.
