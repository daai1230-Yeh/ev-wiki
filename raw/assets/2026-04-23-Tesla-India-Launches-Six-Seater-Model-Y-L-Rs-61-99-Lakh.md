# Tesla India launches six-seater Model Y L at Rs 62 Lakh, targets family SUV market with 681 km range

**Author**: ANI | **Date**: 2026-04-22

---

Tesla India on Wednesday launched the all-new six-seater Model Y L at a starting price of **Rs 61.99 lakh**, marking its most family-focused offering yet as the electric vehicle maker expands its India lineup. Online orders are now open via Tesla's India website, with deliveries set to begin in June 2026 across the country.

The Model Y L is Tesla's first three-row, six-seat configuration for India, designed to maximise interior space and comfort for families.

## Key Specifications

- **Range**: up to 681 km (WLTP)
- **0-100 km/h**: 5.0 seconds
- **Cargo space**: up to 2,539 litres
- **Drivetrain**: All-Wheel Drive (L Premium AWD)
- **Starting price**: Rs 61.99 lakh
- **Monthly payment**: from Rs 49,000

The L Premium AWD is positioned between Tesla's existing trims — the 500 km range RWD at Rs 59.89 lakh and the 661 km Long Range RWD at Rs 67.89 lakh.

## Interior & Features

The second-row captain seats come with powered armrests, one-touch folding, heating and ventilation. The third row gets power recline, one-touch folding and vented climate control. Acoustic glass, updated suspension and 19-inch Machina Wheels aim to deliver a quieter, smoother ride.

Inside: live traffic visualisation, satellite-view maps, live camera feeds and in-car streaming for Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube and Netflix. An 18-speaker + subwoofer Tesla Audio system completes the in-cabin experience.

## Infrastructure Scale-Up

Tesla currently operates **5 Supercharger stations with 20 Superchargers** and 14 Wall Connectors in India. Seven more Supercharger stations are planned on key highways connecting Delhi, Chandigarh, Jaipur, Ahmedabad, Mumbai, Pune, Bengaluru, Hyderabad and Chennai.

Service centres are operational in Mumbai, Gurugram, Delhi and Pune, with new facilities planned in Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai and Ahmedabad.

## Market Position

At Rs 61.99 lakh, the Model Y L undercuts the Long Range RWD variant while adding an extra row of seating and all-wheel drive — a move that could broaden Tesla's appeal beyond urban early adopters to larger families looking for a premium electric SUV.

Customers can experience the Model Y L from April 23 at Tesla's experience centres in BKC Mumbai, Aerocity Delhi and Orchid Business Park Gurugram.
