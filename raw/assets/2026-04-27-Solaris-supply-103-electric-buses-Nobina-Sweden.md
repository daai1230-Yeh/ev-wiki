# Solaris Bus & Coach to supply 103 electric buses to Nobina in Sweden

**Author**: Cora Werwitzke | **Date**: 2026-04-25

---

Solaris has signed additional contracts with the Scandinavian public transport operator Nobina. These include the delivery of a total of 103 Solaris Urbino 15 LE Electric buses. The electric buses will be deployed on routes in the Stockholm, Gothenburg, and Skåne regions. Delivery is planned for 2027.

The Scandinavian public transport operator Nobina is once again expanding its bus fleet in Sweden with battery-electric vehicles from Solaris. This time, the operator has ordered a total of 103 Solaris Urbino 15 LE electric buses through three separate contracts. Fifty of the low-floor electric buses will be deployed in the Stockholm region, 49 in the Skåne region, and four in the Gothenburg area.

The Urbino 15 LE electric buses are designed for both urban and suburban services. Each vehicle is fitted with Solaris High Energy batteries, providing sufficient range for routes extending beyond urban areas. Delivery is scheduled for mid-2027.

This order continues a strong partnership between Solaris and Nobina. Since 2024, Solaris has delivered a total of 201 electric buses to Nobina. Between 2026 and 2027, the bus manufacturer will supply over 320 electric vehicles to the operator across Sweden.

The order also reflects a broader trend in the UK and Nordic markets: in Q1 2026, the UK registered 588 electric buses — a record 37.3% market share of new bus registrations — highlighting the accelerating pace of zero-emission adoption in public transit, in stark contrast to the slower uptake of electric trucks (just 0.9% share in the same period).
