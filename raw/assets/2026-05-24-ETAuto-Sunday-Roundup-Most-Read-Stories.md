Weekly digest of the most-read automotive industry news stories from ETAuto (Economic Times Auto), covering Indian and global automotive trends.

## Most Read Stories This Week

1. **Stellantis to develop new India-made Jeep with Tata for the world** — Tata Motors and Stellantis deepening their 20-year partnership to co-develop a new global Jeep SUV using Tata's platform. Expected export to 50+ countries by 2028.

2. **Honda bets on 10+ models, a local EV, and a hybrid blitz in its India comeback story** — Honda Cars India plans 6+ launches in FY2026-27 including the ZR-V hybrid flagship and a locally manufactured EV.

3. **Can Hero MotoCorp's Vida crack mass EV adoption?** — Analysis of Hero MotoCorp's electric vehicle strategy and whether the Vida brand can achieve mainstream adoption in India.

4. **JLR readies recovery plan to get fit for new 'world order'** — Jaguar Land Rover's restructuring plan amid global automotive disruption.

5. **Top 2W OEMs see single-digit growth this fiscal amid war-led disruptions** — India's top two-wheeler manufacturers facing growth headwinds from West Asia conflict impacts.

6. **As Tata, Stellantis team up for all-new Jeep, a throwback to the alliance journey** — Historical retrospective on the Tata-Fiat/Stellantis partnership spanning 20 years.

7. **Eicher Motors elevates Vinod Aggarwal as Executive Vice-Chairman** — Management changes at Eicher Motors (Royal Enfield parent company).

8. **From monthly price hikes to automation: What component OEMs are doing amid West Asia crisis** — How Indian auto component manufacturers are responding to supply chain disruptions from West Asia conflict.

9. **Royal Enfield plans 2,500 crore manufacturing facility in Andhra Pradesh** — New greenfield plant in Tada, Tirupati district to add production capacity amid strong demand.

10. **India moves past E20, notifies E30 petrol norms to cut oil dependence** — India advancing its ethanol blending program, moving from 20% to 30% ethanol blend targets.
