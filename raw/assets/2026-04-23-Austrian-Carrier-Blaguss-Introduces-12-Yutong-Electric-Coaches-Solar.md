# Austrian carrier Blaguss introduces 12 Yutong electric coaches alongside fleet-wide solar rollout

**Author**: Editorial staff | **Date**: 2026-04-20

---

Austrian transport operator Blaguss has introduced 12 battery-electric coaches alongside ongoing investments in energy infrastructure and solar integration. The company is expanding its "Way to Zero" programme, combining EV deployment across multiple service segments with charging infrastructure and in-house energy production.

## Fleet Electrification Timeline

- **End 2024**: Electric buses deployed in regional public transport in Lower Austria and Burgenland
- **Spring 2025**: Electric hop-on/hop-off buses for tourist services in Vienna
- **2026**: Extension to long-distance and coach operations with 12 **Yutong T12E** electric coaches

The Yutong T12E is specified with **466 kWh** battery capacity and a stated range of up to **500 km**. The vehicles come equipped with rooftop solar panels supporting onboard energy systems. This rollout covers 50 buses across both diesel and electric fleets.

## Solar Pilot Results

Blaguss conducted a six-month solar pilot project across 17 vehicles, reporting:
- Annual diesel savings per bus: **1,616–2,052 litres**
- CO₂ reductions: **4.3–5.5 tonnes per bus annually**
- Estimated payback period: ~**18 months**

Solar panels generate between **500–600 kWh annually**, feeding directly into onboard systems. The solar integration is applied not only to electric buses but also to diesel, HVO and biogas platforms.

## Infrastructure Investment

- PV installations expanded at the company's main site in Vienna and at Vösendorf
- Battery storage and energy management systems being developed
- Surplus energy fed into the public grid
- Fleet-wide solar rollout committed across entire fleet of ~**800 vehicles**

The project is co-financed by national and European funding (BMIMI, EU, FFG).

> "The pilot project delivered clear fuel savings and CO₂ reductions aligned with our corporate objectives. Based on this, the decision was clear: we are now rolling out Green Energy solar systems across our entire fleet as quickly and comprehensively as possible." — Paul Blaguss, Managing Director of Blaguss Group
