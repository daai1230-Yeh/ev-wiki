# Honda bets on 10+ models, a local EV, and a hybrid blitz in its India comeback story

**Date**: 2026-05-22 | **Source**: ETAuto / Economic Times Auto

---

Honda Cars India has confirmed six new model launches for FY2026-27, with a strong focus on hybrid vehicles. Fiscal year 2026-27 is a milestone year for Honda, with six strategic launches including their first BEV and new nameplates from their global lineup with advanced hybrid powertrains.

## Key Models Launching in 2026

Honda's aggressive 2026 India lineup includes City, ZR-V, and Elevate facelifts (launched May 22, 2026), plus the Prelude coupe and the Honda 0 Alpha-based EV in 2027. The ZR-V serves as Honda's new flagship SUV in India, arriving as a completely built import with deliveries from July 2026.

Honda replaced the ZR-V's 1.5-litre turbo petrol engine with a 2.0-litre naturally aspirated strong hybrid unit — the ZR-V is now a hybrid-only model.

## EV Strategy

The next major launch from Honda India is likely to be the Honda 0 Alpha-based EV SUV, which is currently undergoing testing. It will be manufactured at Honda's Tapukara facility in Rajasthan, with an allotted project investment of around Rs 1,200 crore.

## 2028 Expansion

In 2028, Honda will begin introducing strategic models tailored to the Indian market in two vehicle categories: vehicles under 4 meters in length and the mid-size category. Honda aims to strengthen its localisation and reduce its dependence on imports for key components.

## Market Context

To stem its declining market share, Honda Cars India is targeting double-digit growth for the financial year 2026-27. This ambitious plan requires a sharp pivot to hybrids and EVs while maintaining competitive petrol models.
