# Audi's Most Affordable EV Gets More Range, Faster Charging And Updated Tech

**Author**: InsideEVs | **Date**: 2026-04-27

---

The refreshed Audi Q4 E-Tron gets a new range-boosting motor and quicker charging, along with significant technology updates including bidirectional charging capability for the first time in an Audi EV.

## Range Improvements

The longest-range model, the Q4 E-Tron Sportback, now achieves up to 368 miles on the WLTP cycle. The improvements come from a new, 10% more efficient rear motor. The SUV variant gains 19 additional miles over its predecessor.

## Faster Charging

Models equipped with the 82 kWh battery now support up to 185 kW DC fast charging, up from 175 kW. This enables a 10–80% charge in approximately 27 minutes, while up to 115 miles of range can be added in just 10 minutes under optimal conditions.

## Bidirectional Charging (V2L)

For the first time in an Audi EV, the Q4 E-Tron supports Vehicle-to-Load (V2L) bidirectional charging, allowing the battery to supply power to external devices such as e-bikes or camping equipment.

## Technology Updates

The facelift introduces:
- Two new curved screens (11.9- and 12.8-inch), with an optional third 12-inch screen for the passenger
- ChatGPT integration for the voice assistant
- New OLED rear lights
- Additional standard safety technology

The Q4 E-Tron remains Audi's most affordable EV entry point and its bestselling electric model. With up to three screens, bidirectional charging, and nearly 370 miles of range, the refreshed model represents a significant step forward in both practicality and technology.
