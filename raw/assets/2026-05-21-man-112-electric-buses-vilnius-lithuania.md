# MAN secures order for 112 electric buses in Vilnius, Lithuania

**Author**: Editorial staff | **Date**: 2026-05-12

---

MAN Truck & Bus has received an order for **112 fully electric Lion's City E buses** for Vilnius, Lithuania — the largest electric bus order ever recorded in the country. The Lithuanian capital's public transport operator **Vilniaus viešasis transportas (VVT)** signed two contracts:

- **84 × 12-metre solo buses**
- **28 × 18-metre articulated buses**

## Delivery & Specs

- First buses arrive in Vilnius **later in 2026**, with deliveries continuing through end of **2027**
- Supplied locally by **ADAMPOLIS**, MAN's exclusive Lithuanian partner (also providing 5-year technical maintenance)
- Range: up to **380 km** per charge
- Features: air conditioning, passenger information systems, Wi-Fi, and passenger device charging

## MAN Lion's City E Market Position

- Over **3,300 Lion's City E buses** produced and delivered since 2019
- 2025: MAN e-bus sales grew **+118%** YoY — over 1,300 vehicles sold
- ~50% of all MAN city buses sold in Europe now electrically powered
- Q1 2026: MAN delivered ~200 e-buses as bus sales grew 6%
- Production sites: Starachowice (Poland), Ankara (Turkey — newly added), Olifantsfontein (South Africa)

## Context

Vilnius has been aggressively electrifying its fleet. It also commissioned large trolleybus batches from Škoda (2023) and Solaris (2025). This MAN order is the next step in the city's long-term zero-emission transition.

Mayor Valdas Benkunskas: "We are consistently phasing out polluting vehicles and transitioning to cleaner, quieter, and more efficient solutions."

VVT CEO Ignas Degutis: "This is a project of exceptional scale and complexity... we are implementing parallel developments, including charging infrastructure, power capacity, depot upgrades, and new operational models."
