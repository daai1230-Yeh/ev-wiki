Together with my colleagues, Katelyn and Lindsay, we’re pleased to share that Michigan’s Governor, Ms.

1

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
The purpose of her visit is to seek opportunities for economic cooperation and to deepen the 
relationship between Taiwan and Michigan. 

We’d welcome the opportunity to discuss the visit with your team, and there are a few areas where 
we’d like to request your assistance and advice. We’d like to talk about airport arrivals and departures,

airports. Also, we’d like to discuss the possibility of arranging a dinner together on the evening of 9/11 in 
Taipei, including members of Taiwan’s government, Taiwan’s industry, and also our partners at AIT. 

Could you suggest a time or two in the coming days that the relevant members of your team might be 
available to discuss some topics related to the visit with us?
