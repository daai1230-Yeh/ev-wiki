# India EV sales double, mid-market surges as entry segment shrinks

**Author**: ETAuto | **Date**: 2026-04-27

---

FY2026 has emerged as a landmark year for India's electric vehicle industry, with total EV retail sales crossing 2.45 million units — a 25% year-on-year increase compared to 1.97 million units in FY2025.

## Mid-Market Surge

The most striking shift is in the mid-market segment. The share of EVs priced ₹20–30 lakh surged to 35.7% in FY26, up from 24% in FY25 and just 8.6% in FY24. This marks a decisive move upmarket, as the entry-level segment shrinks proportionally.

## Passenger Vehicle Performance

Total electric passenger vehicle retails surged 83.63% to touch 1,99,923 units in FY2026, up from 1,08,873 units in FY2025. Key brand performance:

- **Tata Motors** retained market leadership with 78,811 units (+35.9% YoY)
- **Hyundai Motor India** more than doubled its electric retails (+137.59%) to 5,885 units
- **Kia India** was among the fastest-growing brands, surging 794.26% to 3,738 units from a base of just 418 units

## Electric Two-Wheelers Dominate

India recorded 1.4 million electric two-wheelers in FY2026, capturing 57% of the total EV market. Electric two-wheelers surpassed targets set in the first quarter alone.

## Market Outlook

EV sales are expected to continue accelerating with more products slated to enter the market in the next 18–20 months, according to Mercedes-Benz India's chief. The growth trajectory reflects growing consumer confidence, improved charging infrastructure, and a wider range of models across price segments.
