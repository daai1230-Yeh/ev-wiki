# Electric powerhouse: Mercedes-AMG unveils GT 4-Door Coupé

**Author**: Cora Werwitzke | **Date**: 2026-05-20

---

Mercedes-AMG has unveiled the all-electric GT 4-Door Coupé with up to 860 kW of output and a peak charging capacity of more than 600 kW. Orders for the high-performance model will open in the coming days, initially with the GT 63 and GT 55 powertrain variants.

The all-electric GT 4-Door Coupé is the first model built on the Mercedes-AMG vehicle platform AMG.EA and is powered by three axial flux motors, which combine to deliver up to 860 kW of peak power. Mercedes emphasises that the model embodies 'Affalterbach's pioneering spirit with numerous world firsts.'

Two powertrain versions will be available when orders open: the 63 and 55 versions. Pricing will be based on comparable predecessor vehicles, currently priced at around €179,000 and €158,800, respectively.

## Performance

- 0–100 km/h in 2.1 seconds (GT 63), 2.8 seconds (GT 55)
- 0–200 km/h in 6.4 seconds
- Top speed: 300 km/h (with Driver's Package)
- Three axial flux motors rotating at over 13,000 rpm at top speed

## Battery & Charging

The AMG High Performance Electric Battery (AMG HP.EB) incorporates experience from Formula 1 and the Mercedes-AMG ONE hypercar. Key specs:

- 106 kWh net battery capacity (800V system)
- Energy density: over 298 Wh/kg and 732 Wh/l at cell level
- NCMA cathode chemistry with silicon-containing anode
- 2,660 cylindrical cells in 18 laser-welded modules
- Peak DC charging power: over 600 kW (over 800 ampères)
- 10–80% charge in just 11 minutes
- ~460 km of range recharged in 10 minutes
- Estimated WLTP range: 600–700 km (GT 63), 597–700 km (GT 55)

The battery supports five fast-charging standards: CCS2 (Europe), GB/T (China), CHAdeMO (Japan), CCS1 (South Korea), and NACS (USA).

## Notable Technology

- Axial flux motors used in a production car for the first time
- 800V battery derived from Formula 1 technology with directly cooled cylindrical cells
- Active adaptive aerodynamics with two Venturi plates and rear diffuser
- AMG RACE ENGINEER function for personalised dynamics tuning
- Authentic V8 sound simulation in AMGFORCE S+ mode

CEO Ola Källenius: "It pushes performance to new limits and delivers the emotion our fans expect – now in the electric era."
