# Bosch secures major order from Mercedes-Benz for electric motors

**Author**: electrive | **Date**: 2026-05-19

---

Bosch, the world's largest automotive supplier, has won a large-volume contract to supply electric motors for Mercedes-Benz's next generation of electric drive systems, with production planned to run well into the 2030s as Bosch expands its electric-powertrain role.

Notably, Mercedes will buy only the electric motors from Bosch rather than complete e-axles, pairing them with its own in-house drive units. The automaker already follows this approach on the all-electric CLA, where its proprietary ATS 2.0 drive unit sits at the rear axle and a supplier's motor is used at the front axle on all-wheel-drive variants.
