# Auto China: Mercedes unveils long-wheelbase version of the GLC

**Author**: Sebastian Schaal | **Date**: 23.04.2026 - 15:30

---

Mercedes-Benz unveiled the GLC L EV at Auto China, a version of the electric GLC with an extended wheelbase tailored for the Chinese market - featuring a slightly adapted powertrain.

China-specific long-wheelbase versions of European models have long been a tradition among German automakers, established during the era of internal combustion engines. This practice continues in the age of electromobility, as demonstrated by the latest example from Mercedes-Benz: at Auto China in Beijing, the Stuttgart-based manufacturer unveiled the GLC L EV, the long-wheelbase version of the GLC with EQ technology, which was itself unveiled at the IAA last year.

The GLC L EV measures 4.95 metres in length, an increase of 104 millimetres compared to the standard model. The wheelbase has grown by 55 millimetres to 3.03 metres, while the height remains at 1.71 metres.
