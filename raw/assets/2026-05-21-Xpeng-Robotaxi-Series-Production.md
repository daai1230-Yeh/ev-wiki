# Xpeng launches robotaxi series production

**Author**: electrive | **Date**: 2026-05-19

---

Xpeng, the Chinese Volkswagen partner, has begun series production of a robotaxi at its Guangzhou plant — described as the first robotaxi to be fully developed in China to reach series production.

Key details:

- The vehicle is based on Xpeng's six-seat flagship SUV, the GX, which measures 5.27 metres long.
- It is engineered to Level 4 autonomous-driving standards and runs on four in-house Turing AI chips delivering roughly 3,000 TOPS of onboard compute.
- Xpeng said in late 2025 it intended to bring three robotaxi models to market in 2026.
- Amap (Alibaba Group's mobility app) is named as its first global ecosystem partner for the robotaxi SDK, suggesting Xpeng will operate the vehicles itself while making them bookable through platforms such as Amap or Didi.
