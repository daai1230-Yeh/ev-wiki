# BASF and TSR Group collaborate on battery recycling

**Author**: Cora Werwitzke | **Date**: 15.04.2026 - 15:30

---

BASF and TSR Group, a metal recycling company belonging to the Remondis Group, aim to collaborate on preliminary steps for recycling EV batteries. The duo plans to offer services such as dismantling and discharging used batteries and processing end-of-life batteries into black mass.

To recover valuable materials from end-of-life batteries, the process begins with mechanical steps known in the industry as 'dismantling' and 'discharging'. These involve the professional disassembly and safe discharge of the batteries. BASF and the TSR Group aim to combine their expertise in these steps, as well as in the processing of black mass.
