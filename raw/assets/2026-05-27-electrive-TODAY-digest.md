# electrive TODAY — 27 May 2026

**Source**: electrive global (newsletter@electrive.com)

---

## News
- **Ferrari unveils electric Luce from €550,000** — first BEV; bespoke EV platform, four motors, up to 772 kW; a four-door five-seater rather than a classic two-seat sports car.
- **VW launches ID. Unyx 07 in China** — saloon using Xpeng technology, from 129,900 yuan (~€16,500).
- **Nio boosts Q1 sales and revenue YoY** — but still a sharp decline vs Q4/2025, reflecting the broader Chinese slowdown.
- **India plans major EV incentives for trucks and buses** — programme worth over $1 billion (€862m).
- **Zeron secures further funding for e-truck expansion** — ~$400m raised since March.

## Number of the Day
- IEA: ~1.8 million new public charging points connected worldwide in 2025 (+33% on 2024); global stock now over 7 million.

## Technology
- **Jatco may scrap Sunderland e-axle plant** — Nissan subsidiary reportedly abandons its first European facility amid weak EV demand.
- **DHL Supply Chain opens battery logistics hub in the Netherlands** — Holtum, for high-voltage batteries; operations from early 2027.
- **UK opens permit applications for robotaxi services** — Waymo, Uber, Wayve have announced launches.
- **Mercedes to launch MB.Drive Assist Pro in Germany** — automated door-to-door urban navigation; selected cities end-2026, nationwide early 2027.

## Quotes of the Day
- Tesla VP Lars Moravy: hasn't ruled out a Model S / Model X comeback; prioritising Cybercab, Optimus, Semi.
- Škoda CEO Klaus Zellmer: evaluating 800-volt systems for next-gen platforms (current EVs use 400 V).

## Fleets + Infrastructure
- **Volvo announces Tesla Supercharger access in Europe** — from Q4 2026, 20,000+ Superchargers via the Volvo app.
- **Volvo Buses secures order for 47 battery-electric buses** — Bergkvara, Volvo 8900 Electric for Skåne/Helsingborg.
- **New York delays electric school bus targets** — procurement mandate pushed from 2027 to 2032; full electrification deadline to 2040.
- **New off-grid EV hubs on South Africa's N3** — Zero Carbon Charge commissions two solar-powered HPC stations on the Johannesburg–Durban corridor.

## Service / Short Circuit
- Reading tip: UK Treasury rejected cutting VAT on public EV charging from 20% to 5% (The Guardian).
- Tesla recalls 14,575 Model Ys (Nov 2025–Apr 2026) over missing weight certification labels.
