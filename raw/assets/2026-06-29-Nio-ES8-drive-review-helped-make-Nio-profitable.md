# This Chinese SUV Sold 100,000 Units In Seven Months For A Good Reason

**Author**: Kevin Williams | **Date**: 2026-06-29

---

Kevin Williams drove the **third-generation Nio ES8** and came away impressed enough to say the vehicle helped make Nio profitable.

## Key Facts

- **Sales**: 100,000 units sold in the first 7 months on sale; 120,000+ in year one total
- The ES8 is Nio's flagship SUV — a large 6/7-seat premium electric SUV
- The vehicle's quality and features helped Nio turn profitable after years of losses

## Context

InsideEVs has been covering Chinese EVs in depth, including the Xiaomi SU7 (also reviewed by Kevin Williams in June 2026). The Nio ES8's success reflects the strong premium Chinese EV segment competing with established luxury brands.
