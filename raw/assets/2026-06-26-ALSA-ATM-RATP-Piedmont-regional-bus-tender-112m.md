# ALSA, ATM Milano and RATP among contenders for Piedmont's €112m/year regional bus tender

**Author**: Editorial staff | **Date**: 2026-06-26

---

**Spain's ALSA**, **ATM Milano**, and **RATP** are set to compete for Piedmont (Italy)'s regional bus services tender, one of the largest regional public transport procurements in Italy.

## Tender Overview

- **Procuring body**: AMP (Agenzia della Mobilità Piemontese), Northwestern Italy (capital: Turin)
- **Scope**: ~**56.1 million vehicle-km/year** across 8 lots covering 4 transport basins
- **Annual compensation**: ~**€112.4 million**
- Status: Qualification phase completed; administrative verification underway before competitive bidding

## Lots & Basins

- **Lots 1–2** (South-West / Cuneo): 16.1M veh-km, €31.9M/yr
- **Lots 3–4** (South-East / Asti + Alessandria): 13.8M veh-km, €28.6M/yr
- **Lots 5–6** (North-East / Biella, Vercelli, Novara, VCO): 7.5M veh-km, €14.1M/yr
- **Lots 7–8** (Metropolitan / Turin area): 18.7M veh-km, €37.8M/yr

## Qualified Competitors by Class

**Class A3** (whole procurement): ATM Milano, Air Pullman, STAC, ALSA, Nex Continental  
**Class A2** (up to 3 lots): VITA, Autostradale, VCO Trasporti; plus incumbents GTT, Cavourese, Bus Company, RATP Dev Italia, Busitalia  
**Class A1** (single lot): Local operators including Baranzelli, Gherra, SAV, ACTP, and others
