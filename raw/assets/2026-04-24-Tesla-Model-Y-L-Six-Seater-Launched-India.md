# Tesla launches six-seater Model Y L in India to revive sluggish demand

**Author**: ETAuto | **Date**: April 22, 2026

---

Tesla launched a new six-seater version of its Model Y in India on April 22, 2026, in an effort to accelerate sluggish sales in the local electric vehicle market. The Model Y L — a six-seat, long-wheelbase configuration that made its global debut in China — is Tesla's first new product in India since the company's market entry in the country.

The variant features a three-row seating layout for families seeking more space than the standard five-seat Model Y, which Tesla began selling in India last July.

**Key specifications:**
- Range: up to 681 km (WLTP cycle)
- Acceleration: 0-100 km/h in 5.0 seconds
- Cargo space: up to 2,539 litres
- Starting price: ₹61.99 lakh (~$74,000)
- Deliveries: starting June 2026

Customers can experience the Model Y L starting April 23, 2026 at Tesla locations in BKC (Mumbai), Aerocity (Delhi), and Orchid Business Park (Gurugram).

The Elon Musk-led EV maker aims to revive traction in the world's third-largest auto market, where sales have been tepid since its entry. The long-wheelbase configuration is expected to appeal to Indian families who value multi-row seating and generous interior space.
