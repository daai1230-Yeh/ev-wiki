# The Mobility House, EcoG and EV-Tech unveil BiDi Wallbox

**Author**: Florian Treiss | **Date**: 18.06.2026

---

German companies The Mobility House and EcoG have joined forces with Chinese manufacturer EV-Tech to develop a DC wallbox supporting Vehicle-to-Grid (V2G) and Vehicle-to-Home (V2H).

**Product**: ChargeLine BiDi — 800V DC wallbox, 11 kW output. Supports ISO 15118-20, OCPP 2.1, and earlier versions (ISO 15118-2, OCPP 2.01, OCPP 1.6). Open standards — any V2G-capable EV can connect, any OCPP 2.1 backend can integrate.

"This lays the foundation for using millions of vehicle batteries as decentralised energy storage systems, balancing peak loads in the grid, and enabling EV drivers to benefit significantly from active participation in the energy market," — Thomas Raffeiner, CEO of The Mobility House.

The ChargeLine BiDi will be exhibited at Power2Drive Munich (23–26 June 2026) at EcoG's booth (B6.130, with live VW ID.3 demo) and The Mobility House's booth (B6.510).

**Context**: In April, The Mobility House announced a free home-charging offering where EV batteries act as grid storage. This V2G electricity tariff is planned for Germany later in 2026 for the Renault 5 and new Mercedes-Benz electric GLC.
