# Hyundai Motor and TVS Motor sign JDA for electric three-wheelers

**Source**: ETAuto / Google Alerts | **Date**: 2026-04-21

---

Hyundai Motor Company and TVS Motor Company have signed a Joint Development Agreement (JDA) to advance the development and commercialisation of innovative electric three-wheeler (E3W) solutions, specifically designed to meet India's last-mile mobility needs.

The partnership aims to leverage Hyundai's global EV technology expertise with TVS Motor's deep understanding of India's three-wheeler market and manufacturing capabilities. The E3W solution is proposed to be produced collaboratively in India, while both parties are also exploring global export opportunities.

This announcement follows Hyundai's unveiling of E3W and E4W electric last-mile mobility concepts at India's Auto Expo 2025. The partnership represents a significant step in India's electrification of commercial and last-mile transport, a segment where adoption has been growing rapidly supported by government subsidies.

India has extended subsidies for electric two-wheelers and three-wheelers in 2026, further supporting the market opportunity for this collaboration.
