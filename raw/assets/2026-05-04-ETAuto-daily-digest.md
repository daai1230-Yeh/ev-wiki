### Auto Inc may take ₹25,000 crore hit on end-of-life vehicle rules
A clause under Rule 4(6) of end-of-life vehicle regulations has raised concerns after auditors flagged its implications. Industry executives estimate the auto sector could face a ₹25,000 crore financial impact.

### Hyundai slips as Tata Motors, Mahindra race ahead in calendar 2026
India's homegrown carmakers Tata Motors and Mahindra are firing on all cylinders, maintaining a healthy lead over Hyundai as domestic market share shifts in 2026.

### Maruti Suzuki India starts FY27 with market share increase to 42%
Maruti achieved record domestic sales of 1,91,122 units in April 2026, driven by strong performance across segments. The company's market share rose to 42% at the start of FY2027.

### EVs moving at faster speed in premium lane
Electric vehicles are gaining momentum particularly in the premium segment, outpacing adoption in the mass market.

### EV sales defy seasonal dip; April clocks second-highest volumes
April 2026 EV sales recorded the second-highest monthly volumes in India despite seasonal headwinds, indicating sustained consumer demand.

### AC electric double-decker bus rolls out in Maharashtra
A new AC electric double-decker bus has been introduced in Maharashtra, adding to India's growing fleet of electric public transport.
