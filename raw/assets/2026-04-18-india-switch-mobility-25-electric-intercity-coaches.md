# India: Switch Mobility to deploy 25 electric intercity coaches

**Author**: Sagar Parikh | **Date**: 2026-04-18

---

Hinduja Group-owned e-bus and e-LCV manufacturer Switch Mobility plans to deploy 25 electric intercity coaches in India. Purple Bus, a brand of Prasanna Purple Mobility Solutions, is procuring these e-buses.

Switch Mobility flagged off the first batch of electric intercity coaches on the Mumbai–Pune route, a major travel corridor in Maharashtra. Ten units were delivered first, with the remaining vehicles to follow later this year.

This marks the first time Switch Mobility has commissioned intercity coaches for a private company, and the first time Purple Bus has deployed electric buses on an intercity route.

Switch Mobility CEO Ganesh Mani stated: "The flag-off marks another important step in expanding electric mobility beyond cities and strengthening our presence in the intercity segment. This partnership reflects the growing confidence in electric buses for high-frequency routes like Mumbai–Pune, where performance, reliability, and efficiency are critical."

Prasanna Purple Mobility Solutions CMD Prasanna Patwardhan added: "Entering the intercity segment with electric mobility is a significant milestone for us. Through this collaboration, we are introducing a cleaner and more efficient travel option on the Mumbai–Pune route."

Switch Mobility is fulfilling the order using the EiV12, a 12-metre model available in four versions: intercity, low-floor, staff, and tarmac. The intercity version is 12 m long, 2.6 m wide, 3.48 m tall, with a 6.4 m wheelbase, 1.05 m floor height, 45 pushback seats, and air suspension at both ends.

**Key specs of the EiV12 intercity version:**
- Motor: 235 kW permanent magnet synchronous motor
- Battery: 423 kWh LFP pack, charged via CCS2 dual-gun charger
- Max speed: 80 km/h
- Range: more than 300 km per charge
- Charging time: 2–3 hours

In a concurrent development, Switch Mobility also commissioned a fleet of EiV12 tarmac-version buses at Navi Mumbai International Airport. The company has deployed more than 2,500 electric buses and over 2,500 electric LCVs to date.
