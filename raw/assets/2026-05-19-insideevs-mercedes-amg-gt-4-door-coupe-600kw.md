# Mercedes-Benz's Electric Super Sedan Has A New Kind Of EV Motor And Unprecedented Charging Speed

**Author**: Suvrat Kothari | **Date**: 2026-05-19

---

The new Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe may be the most technologically advanced electric vehicle any Western automaker has put into production yet. It uses a new kind of EV motor to reduce weight and improve performance, a bespoke battery pack infused with lessons from Formula 1, and a peak charging power of 600 kilowatts — the most from any non-Chinese automaker.

**Axial Flux Motors (World First in a Production EV):** The AMG GT is the first production EV to use axial flux motors (from YASA, acquired by Mercedes in 2021). Unlike conventional "soup can" radial flux motors, YASA's design is pancake-shaped with two disc-shaped rotors sandwiching the stator — delivering higher torque, better packaging efficiency, and greater power density. Mercedes says the power unit is 67% lighter and 67% shorter than conventional motors.

- **GT 63 trim**: 3 motors (2 rear, 1 front) → 1,153 hp / 1,475 lb-ft torque, 0–60 mph in 2.3 sec, 0–124 mph in 6.4 sec, top speed 186 mph
- **GT 55 trim**: 805 hp / 1,328 lb-ft torque

**Silicon Anode Battery:** 106 kWh pack, 2,660 cylindrical cells in 18 modules. Cells are 4.1" tall / 1" wide (optimised for cooling). Chemistry: NCMA cathode + silicon-containing anode. Cell energy density: 298 Wh/kg. WLTP range: ~700 km (~315 miles EPA estimated).

**600 kW Charging:** Fastest among non-Chinese automakers. 10–80% SoC in just 11 minutes. Adds ~285 miles WLTP range (≈250 EPA miles) in 10 minutes. For comparison: Tesla Cybertruck does 500 kW; Lucid Gravity does 400 kW; BYD's latest does 10–97% in 9 minutes.

**Timeline:** GT 55 arrives at US dealerships later 2026; GT 63 follows in early 2027. Pricing TBA — expected to be very high given the technology.

**Design note:** The article describes the exterior as polarising — gaudy DRLs with Mercedes star graphics, large headlamp housings, vertical illuminated grille slats, circular taillights. Owners also get a simulated V8 sound using 1,600 audio files.
