# Caltrans orders 524 additional EVs from Rivian

**Date**: 2026-06-15

---

The California Department of Transportation (Caltrans) has expanded its EV order with Rivian, bringing the total to 977 vehicles worth approximately $97 million (~€84 million).

The new batch includes 201 R1S SUVs and 323 R1T pick-ups ($52.7 million). Caltrans had already ordered 453 vehicles from Rivian in June 2025. This is reportedly the largest known fleet agreement for Rivian outside its van programme with Amazon.

The orders were placed under a nationwide framework agreement for alternative-drive vehicles, driven partly by California regulations requiring zero-emission vehicles to account for at least half of new light-duty service vehicle procurements. By August 2025, Rivian had sold around 700 vehicles to public institutions; the latest 524-vehicle Caltrans order alone surpasses most previous public sector contracts.
