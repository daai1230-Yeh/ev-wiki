# Sustainable Bus Weekly – May 20, 2026

**Source**: Sustainable Bus Weekly (info@sustainable-bus.com) | **Edition**: May 20th, 2026

---

This week's edition features news from the Next Mobility Exhibition 2026 (NME 2026) held May 13–16 at Fiera Milano Rho, Italy, along with major bus industry news from across Europe.

## Top Stories

**Menarini debuts new e-bus range at Next Mobility Exhibition 2026** — World premiere of Menarini's latest electric bus lineup at NME 2026 in Milan. *(Feature story of the week)*

**Yutong at NME 2026: Battery-electric buses & fleet digitalization** — Yutong showcased four battery-electric buses and EV technologies with a focus on long-life components, integrated electric architecture, and fleet digitalization.

**VinFast European CV strategy: Software ownership & vertical integration** — Interview with Diego Ghirardi, Head of Commercial Vehicles, on VinFast's European market approach. The company sees software ownership and vertical integration as key competitive advantages in the European electric bus market.

**MAN secures order for 112 electric buses in Vilnius, Lithuania** — Major order win for MAN electric buses in the Lithuanian capital.

**IVECO Bus electric buses for TCL network electrification** — IVECO Bus delivers electric buses for French TCL network fleet electrification.

## More This Week

**Zenobe finances over 280 electric buses under new framework agreement with Go-Ahead** — Major financing deal supporting large-scale electric bus fleet deployment across the Go-Ahead Group.

**Irizar ie bus Efficient debut for Italian market at NME 2026** — Irizar showcased the ie bus Efficient model with Italian market debut at the Next Mobility Exhibition.

**CharterUP targets campus mobility and autonomous driving** — Interview with VP New Mobility Stephen Joos on the company's strategy for campus EV mobility and autonomous shuttle opportunities.

**Busitalia deploys first Karsan hydrogen buses in Terni** — Italian carrier Busitalia (Ferrovie dello Stato group) puts first Karsan hydrogen fuel cell buses into service in Terni, central Italy.

**Solaris at NME 2026: Urbino 10.5 electric** — Solaris showcased the compact Urbino 10.5 electric model at Milan's Next Mobility Exhibition 2026.

**Solaris management update** — Riccardo Cornetto appointed as Italy's General Manager; Alberto Fiore moves to commercial leadership role.
