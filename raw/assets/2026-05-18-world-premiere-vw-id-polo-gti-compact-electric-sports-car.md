# World premiere: VW ID. Polo GTI – a compact electric sports car with major impact?

**Author**: Daniel Bönnighausen | **Date**: 2026-05-15

---

Volkswagen is bringing the GTI badge into the electric era with the ID. Polo GTI, featuring 166 kW, an adaptive chassis, and a front-axle differential lock. Pre-sales are expected to start in autumn at prices just below €39,000.

## Key Specs

- **Power**: 166 kW (APP290 electric motor, front-wheel drive)
- **Top speed**: 175 km/h
- **Battery**: 52 kWh NMC (LFP not available in GTI)
- **Range (WLTP)**: up to 424 km
- **DC charging**: up to 105 kW (10–80% in ~24 minutes)
- **AC charging**: 11 kW onboard charger (~5.5 hours for full charge)
- **Price**: starting just below €39,000 (pre-sales opening autumn 2026)
- **Length**: ~4.10 m (4.3 cm longer than standard ID. Polo)

## Design & Heritage

The ID. Polo GTI marks 50 years after the debut of the first GTI. The GTI name, previously used for combustion-engine cars (where 'I' stood for 'Injection'), now stands for 'Intelligence'. VW brand chief Thomas Schäfer confirmed the GTI name only applies when the car truly delivers on the GTI promise.

Visual GTI cues include the signature red stripe at the front, honeycomb air intake, tartan seat pattern (reinterpreted in white/grey/red), red decorative stitching throughout, and 19-inch alloy wheels. The GTI sits 1.7 cm lower than the standard ID. Polo.

## Chassis & Driving Dynamics

Built on the MEB+ platform, the GTI features:
- MacPherson front axle + compound-link rear axle
- Electronically controlled DCC adaptive damper system (on both axles)
- Standard front-axle differential lock (as used in Golf GTI)
- Dedicated **GTI driving profile** (activated via steering wheel button), maximizing motor response, progressive steering, and DCC sport settings
- Launch Control when GTI mode active

## Interior

GTI-specific interior touches: red/black color scheme throughout, tartan pattern on sports seats, GTI emblem in headrests, red 12 o'clock marker on steering wheel. Functionality (displays, infotainment, space) is identical to standard ID. Polo.

## Significance

The ID. Polo GTI is the first battery-electric Volkswagen to carry the iconic GTI badge. Whether it lives up to the GTI name in driving dynamics awaits a first test drive — but the hardware (adaptive chassis, differential lock, dedicated GTI mode) suggests VW is serious about the badge.
