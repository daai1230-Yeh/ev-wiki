# EV sales defy seasonal dip; April 2026 clocks second-highest volumes

**Author**: ETAuto | **Date**: May 4, 2026

---

India's electric passenger vehicle industry delivered 23,163 units in April 2026, with 17 manufacturers of electric SUVs and MPVs achieving 73% year-on-year growth. This was the second-highest monthly retail sales on record, after March 2026 (23,749 units).

## Market Share Breakdown (April 2026)

- **Tata Motors** — 8,506 units (+77% YoY) → 37% market share. Maintained market leadership.
- **Mahindra** — 5,394 units (+63% YoY) → 24% market share. Outsold JSW MG Motor for second consecutive month.
- **JSW MG Motor India** — 4,978 units (+32% YoY) → 22% market share.
- **VinFast** — 1,231 units (first time crossing 1,000 units since deliveries began in October 2025).

## Sector Highlights

April's strong EV performance defied the typical seasonal slowdown at the start of a new financial year. The result signals sustained consumer momentum in the Indian EV market, driven by expanding model choices in the mid-market segment.

India's EV market continues to see mid-market growth as the entry segment shrinks — a sign that buyers are upgrading to higher-specification electric vehicles.
