# electrive LIVE: V2G in action — First live demo of bidirectional charging for trucks

**Source**: electrive | **Event date**: 2026-04-24

---

On 24 April 2026, the SPIRIT-E consortium will publicly demonstrate bidirectional charging with a heavy-duty battery-electric truck for the first time. electrive will broadcast the event live from Spedition Schmid in Obertraubling near Regensburg, Germany.

The event will feature keynote speeches, expert discussions, and live demonstrations of:
- Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V) charging
- Vehicle-to-Depot (V2Depot / V2S)
- Vehicle-to-Grid (V2G)

All demonstrations take place on the freight forwarding company's premises.

**About SPIRIT-E:** The SPIRIT-E research project is funded by the German Federal Ministry for Economic Affairs and Energy (BMWi). It is supported by eight partners: the Technical University of Munich, MAN Truck & Bus, TenneT, SBRS (Shell), Fraunhofer IEE, the Research Center for Energy Economics (FfE), Hubject, and Consolinno Energy. The project focuses on the potential of battery-electric trucks as flexible energy storage solutions in the energy transition.

The event is presented in German, with AI-powered simultaneous translation into English, Spanish, and French for international guests.
