October 23, 2024

- One of the leading manufacturers of minibuses in Europe, Vilnius ALTAS AUTO has expanded its

range of electric buses with the fourth model - the 35-passenger Altas Novus City V7.

- The bus will be attractive to transporters in Lithuanian cities because it is extremely functional,

reliable, compact and at the same time spacious.

- The bus, which meets the strict EU safety requirements, marks a new stage in the development of
ALTAS AUTO - the company is entering the segment of larger capacity buses, which is growing
rapidly in Europe today.

ALTAS AUTO took this decisive step in strategic cooperation with Zhongtong Bus, one of the world's
largest manufacturers of electric buses, which has accumulated 15 years of experience. experience in
the production of electric buses. After signing a cooperation agreement a year ago, ALTAS AUTO and
Zhongtong Bus jointly created a product that meets European requirements and quality standards. 

"Zhongtong Bus" will supply Lithuanians with a bus body with the main elements - integrated electric
drive, batteries, chassis. The production of the ALTAS AUTO bus will be ﬁnished according to the
solutions developed by Lithuanian engineers, adapted to Europe - the interior, audiovisual monitoring
and safety systems will be integrated here, an ergonomic driver's workplace will be installed, and other
solutions that meet EU safety requirements.

This strategic partnership between the two companies will allow ALTAS AUTO to
develop a new series of medium-sized buses in the future, which will consistently
meet the increasingly strict EU safety and quality requirements.
