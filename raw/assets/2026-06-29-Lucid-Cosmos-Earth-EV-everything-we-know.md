# Lucid Cosmos And Earth: Everything We Know About The Tesla Model Y Rivals

**Author**: Tim Levin | **Date**: 2026-06-29 (Newsletter: June 29, 2026)

---

Lucid has confirmed its first midsize electric SUV called **Cosmos** during its **Investor Day 2026** event in New York City. The Cosmos will rival the Tesla Model Y and Rivian R2.

## Key Specifications

- **Price**: Expected to start around **$50,000**
- **Architecture**: 800-volt battery system with next-gen centralized architecture
- **Charging**: Bidirectional charging capable; adds **200 miles of range in 14 minutes**
- **Design**: Coupe-like roofline, curvaceous nose, illuminated Lucid emblems at both ends

## Production Timeline

Initial production will begin in **Saudi Arabia by the end of 2026**. Early production batches will head to the U.S. Lucid also announced the related **Earth** model (a roomier variant).

## Context

Lucid laid off 18% of workers and cut Arizona production as part of restructuring. The Cosmos/Earth platform represents a critical next chapter — Lucid needs a volume product to scale beyond the ultra-premium Air sedan.
