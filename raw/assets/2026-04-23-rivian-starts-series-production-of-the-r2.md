# Rivian starts series production of the R2

**Author**: Sebastian Schaal | **Date**: 23.04.2026 - 11:45

---

Rivian has started series production of its mid-sized electric SUV, the R2, at its plant in Normal, Illinois. The vehicle is currently available for order starting at $57,990, with the first customer deliveries scheduled for spring.

The first customer-specification units of the Rivian R2 have now rolled off the production line at the Normal plant. However, these units are still undergoing final quality controls and internal validation checks.

"We are on track to begin handing over keys to our first customers later this spring," the company stated. By the end of the year, Rivian aims to ramp up production significantly.
