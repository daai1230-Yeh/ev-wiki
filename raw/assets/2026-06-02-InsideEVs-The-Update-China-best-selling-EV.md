**I Drove China's Best-Selling Car. It's On Top For A Reason**
The Geely EX2 (Xingyuan) outsells everything Tesla and BYD make in China, with nearly half a million sales last year. Kevin Williams drove the EX2 in China and found it has cutting-edge software, independent rear suspension, and rear-wheel drive — feeling far from cheap.

**I Took A Hyundai Ioniq 5 EV On A Roadtrip. It Made Gas Feel Financially Irresponsible**
An electric road trip in a Hyundai Ioniq 5 showed how EVs can help beat high gas prices, even when relying on pricey fast chargers.

**Elon Musk Promised 1,000 Texas Robotaxis Last Year. It's Nowhere Near That**
The official Tesla Robotaxi count was revealed for the first time in registration information submitted to the Texas DMV, falling far short of Musk's earlier promise.

**Rivian Says Fight Over Apple CarPlay Is 'Completely Obsolete' Thanks To AI**
Rivian says rapid progress in AI-powered voice assistants will make Apple CarPlay a thing of the past.
