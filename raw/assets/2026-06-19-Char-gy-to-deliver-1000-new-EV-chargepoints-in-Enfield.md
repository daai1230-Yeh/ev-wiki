# Char.gy to deliver 1000 new EV chargepoints in Enfield

**Author**: Ciaran Daly | **Date**: 18.06.2026

---

The London Borough of Enfield is gaining 1,000 new EV charging points via Char.gy, primarily retrofitted to lampposts with additional standalone chargers at five sites. Focus: low-cost overnight and long-stay charging for residents without off-street parking.

Char.gy will fit chargers directly into streetlight columns. Similar deployments use the 'Flow' range — small box-shaped Type 2 units mountable on lampposts within 2 hours. Specs: 5 kW and 7 kW variants, contactless/app payment, ISO 15118 (Plug & Charge). Residents can submit location requests via a council form.

"Access to convenient, affordable charging is key to helping residents make the switch to electric vehicles. By expanding our on-street charging network, we're making EV ownership a realistic option for far more people." — Councillor Maria Alexandrou, Enfield Council.
