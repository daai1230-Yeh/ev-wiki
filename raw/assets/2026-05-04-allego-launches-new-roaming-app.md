# Allego launches new roaming app

**Author**: Sebastian Schaal | **Date**: 2026-05-01

---

Dutch charging provider Allego has released a new version of its app that integrates competitors' charging networks, giving users access to one million charging points across Europe through a single app.

## Key Features

- **Roaming access**: Start charging sessions at other operators' charging points directly through the Allego app — no additional fees for using third-party providers
- **Smart Route Planner**: Plan charging stops across all networks on long-distance trips
- **Price transparency**: Allego passes on B2B prices without markups; prices vary by operator (e.g. EnBW €0.79/kWh, Aral Pulse €0.96/kWh without subscription)

## Allego Pricing (Germany)

| Type | Standard | App discount (9%) | Plus subscription (€9.99/mo) |
|------|----------|-------------------|-------------------------------|
| Ultra-fast (HPC) | €0.76/kWh | €0.69/kWh | €0.49/kWh (-36%) |
| Fast chargers | €0.655/kWh | — | — |
| AC chargers | €0.60/kWh | — | — |

Plus subscription breaks even at ~370 km/month (18 kWh/100km avg consumption).

## Allego Network in Germany

- 525 ultra-fast charging locations
- 2,355 charging points
- Plans to add 100 more stations by end of 2026

Example savings (Hamburg–Munich): Standard €102.60 → App discount €93.15 → Plus subscription €66.15.
