**My Dog Is Now Obsessed With Chevy EVs — And It's All Thanks To The Weird Space-Ship Hum They Make**
Thanks to the federally mandated pedestrian warning sounds required for EVs and hybrids, Mack Hogan's dog Kickflip has become a certified Chevy enthusiast. The spaceship-like hum of the Chevy Blazer EV sends Flip into a frenzy — he now associates the pedestrian alert sound with his favorite things (the beach, his human coming home), and reacts to any Chevy EV that passes on a walk.

**It's Official: Rivian Will Start Delivering R2s In Two Weeks**
Order books open and test drives start in just two weeks.

**Waymo Says Its Next-Gen Ojai Robotaxis Are Ready For Prime Time**
You'll soon be able to ride in the high-tech Zeekr-built vans, which Waymo is using to expand its robotaxi fleet.

**Ferrari Could Have Faked A V12. The Luce EV Does Something Stranger**
Ferrari chose not to simulate the sound of a combustion engine in the Luce, embracing its electric nature instead.
