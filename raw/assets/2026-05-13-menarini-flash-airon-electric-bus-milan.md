# Menarini unveils Flash and Airon new electric bus models in Milan

**Author**: Editorial Staff | **Date**: 2026-05-13

---

Menarini (owned by Seri Industrial) presented its **Flash** and **Airon** new electric bus platforms at Next Mobility Exhibition 2026 in Milan — displaying the 8-meter and 12-meter versions respectively. The company is also relaunching its ambition to expand on international markets.

Both platforms feature full low-floor architecture and roof-mounted battery systems, developed for different urban operational segments.

---

## Menarini Flash — Compact Urban Bus (8–9 m)

**Available lengths**: 8.258 m and 9.443 m

- **Structure**: Self-supporting monocoque body, high-strength tubular steel frame, full immersion cathodic anti-corrosion treatment
- **Suspension**: Independent front suspension, rear portal axle, air suspension with kneeling function
- **Motor**: Permanent magnet synchronous motor (Accelera by Cummins / formerly Siemens), rear longitudinal mount — **290 kW peak / 3,000 Nm**
- **Battery**: BorgWarner NMC modules, up to **392 kWh**, liquid cooling with thermal pre-conditioning
- **Range**: >300 km (8 m) / >400 km (9 m)

---

## Menarini Airon — Standard Urban Bus (10–12 m)

**Available lengths**: 10.620 m and 12.100 m (12-meter: 5,900 mm wheelbase, 2,550 mm wide, 3,200 mm tall)

- **Structure**: Monocoque with tubular steel frame; produced at Flumeri plant
- **Suspension**: Independent front, electronically controlled ECAS air suspension, continuously controlled damping
- **Braking**: Regenerative braking + EBS, ABS, ASR, electronic parking brake
- **Motor**: Rear longitudinal PMSM — **290 kW peak / 3,000 Nm** (same as Flash)
- **Battery**: Lithium-NMC up to **490 kWh**
- **Range**: >310 km (10 m) / >480 km (12 m)
- **Capacity**: Up to 22 seated passengers (12 m)

**Both platforms include**: ESC, TPMS, acoustic reverse warning, alcohol interlock preparation.
