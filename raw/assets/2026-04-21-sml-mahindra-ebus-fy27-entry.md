# SML Mahindra to foray into e-bus segment in FY27

**Source**: ETAuto Latest News | **Date**: 2026-04-21

---

SML Isuzu (formerly SML Mahindra) has announced plans to foray into the electric bus segment in FY2027, aiming to tap the policy-led opportunity created by India's growing push for electric public transport.

The company said it will assess participation in ongoing government tenders depending on commercial returns, reflecting a cautious but strategic entry into the e-bus market. India's government has been aggressively pushing electric bus adoption through programmes such as PM e-Bus Sewa, which aims to deploy thousands of electric buses across cities.

SML Mahindra's entry would add another domestic manufacturer to a market that already includes Tata Motors, Olectra, Switch Mobility, and PMI Electro Mobility, among others.

The move aligns with a broader trend of Indian commercial vehicle manufacturers entering the EV space as policy support, falling battery costs, and growing operator confidence in electric buses make the segment commercially attractive.
