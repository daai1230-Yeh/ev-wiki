# Introducing the Škoda Epiq – the beloved compact SUV

**Author**: electrive | **Date**: 2026-05-19

---

Škoda has detailed the Epiq, the third model in the Volkswagen Group's new family of compact electric cars alongside the VW ID. Polo and the Cupra Raval. The battery-electric SUV combines roomy interior packaging, practical features and bidirectional charging.

Key facts:

- **Pricing**: A headline starting price of €25,900 acts as an introductory loss-leader; for now the Epiq is orderable from €32,100.
- **Size/class**: Classified as a compact car at 4.17 metres long, short enough to qualify for the planned European M1e small-car class.
- **Variants**: Three power levels from 85 to 155 kW — the Epiq 35 (85 kW) and Epiq 40 (99 kW) use a 38.5 kWh LFP battery, while the top Epiq 55 (155 kW) gets a 55 kWh NMC battery.
- **Practicality**: 475-litre boot plus a 25-litre frunk.
- **Bidirectional charging**: All variants are prepared for it, ranging from powering external devices to acting as grid storage, though suitable tariffs and hardware aren't yet available.
