## 電動車+泰國 (EV + Thailand)

- **Honda增產eHEV新休旅車** (墨新聞): Honda Thailand is expanding eHEV SUV production. HR-V eHEV starts at THB 959,000; CR-V targets family SUV market. Thailand's auto sector faces pressure from EV price competition and weak domestic demand.
- **Thailand PM switches to BYD**: Thai PM Anutin changed his official vehicle from Rolls-Royce to BYD electric, and called on Thai citizens to use EVs for environmental reasons. Bangkok continues to experience heavy traffic while Thailand faces an energy crisis.
- **Thai "tuk-tuk" drivers struggling** as fuel prices rise amid energy shortage.

## 電動巴士 (Electric Bus)

- **台美氫能技術合作** (Yahoo新聞 / 蕃新聞): Taiwan-US hydrogen bus technology cooperation. Hydrogen buses are seen as a key component for Taiwan's "2030 full electrification of public buses" goal, especially for long-distance and heavy-load routes. Current TCO of hydrogen buses remains significantly higher than BEV buses: CAPEX ~NT$18-25 million/bus vs lower for BEV.
- **工研院扶植「起而行綠能」** (中華日報): Taiwan's ITRI (Industrial Technology Research Institute) is supporting startup "Rise Green Energy" for Southeast Asia expansion. Electric buses, once fully charged, can serve as mobile grid storage (V2G), contributing to grid resilience.

## 電動車+韓國 (EV + Korea)

- **現代汽車電池訂閱新模式** (Highmotor): Hyundai launched Korea's first battery subscription pilot — buying an EV without owning the battery. New regulation (approved Nov 2025) allows separate registration of vehicle body and battery for the first time. → *See separate saved article.*
- **比亞迪韓國市場擴張**: BYD expanding rapidly in Korea; European press noting "China can now make luxury cars too." BYD targeting global markets beyond China despite US market restrictions.
- **美國電動車型停產**: Dozens of EV models discontinued in the US due to tariffs, loss of tax credits, and import cost restructuring. Example: Hyundai Kona Electric (~$33,000 starting price, one of cheapest in US market) discontinued in 2026 — Hyundai could not justify shipping from Korean factory at 25% import tariff.

## 電動車+印度 (EV + India)

- **Toyota印度三廠擴建** (日經中文網): Toyota plans 3 new assembly plants in Maharashtra, ~$1.91B investment, capacity to 1 million units by 2030s. India to become Toyota's 4th largest production base. → *See separate saved article.*
- **中國車企海外收購**: Analyst Tang Jin (湯進) notes Chinese automakers are entering a transition phase in electrification and their next step is overseas acquisitions. Suzuki holds a unique competitive advantage in India against Chinese EV makers.

## 電動車+中國大陸 (EV + China)

- **中國電動車企期待川習會** (經濟日報): Chinese EV companies hope a Trump-Xi meeting will open markets and enable two-way cooperation. BYD's Li Ke (interviewed by CNN) states BYD's goal is the global market, not just China.
- **比亞迪豪車市場進軍**: "Europeans amazed: China can make luxury cars too." BYD expanding aggressively; entered Korean market in just 11 months. BYD not relying on the US market but targeting global expansion.
