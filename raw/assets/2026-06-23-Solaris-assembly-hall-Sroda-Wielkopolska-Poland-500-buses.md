# Solaris opens new assembly hall in Poland, adding capacity for 500 buses

**Author**: Editorial staff | **Date**: 2026-06-23

---

**Solaris Bus & Coach** has opened a **new final assembly hall** in **Środa Wielkopolska** (~35 km southeast of Poznań), adding ~**500 buses/year** capacity and announcing a second factory (intercity buses) planned for **2029**.

## Key Facts

- New hall adds **final assembly** to existing steel-frame welding operations at Środa (active since 1998)
- Complements the main Bolechowo campus (north of Poznań)
- Annual output target: **2,000 vehicles/year** — nearly reachable given 2025 delivery of **1,631 buses** (86% low/zero emission)
- **~300 direct jobs** created; several hundred more indirect positions via regional suppliers
- Solaris **ranked 2nd in EU zero-emission bus registrations in Q1 2026**

## Future Plans

- **Second factory** (intercity bus dedicated) in Środa Wielkopolska municipality
- **47 hectares** of land already secured; target operational: **2029**
- New intercity bus platform expected late 2026/early 2027
- **North America expansion**: first orders being fulfilled (US + Canada); dedicated models to launch at APTA expo Chicago, October 2026

CEO Agata Stańda: "We are investing in expanding our production capacity and product portfolio to meet the growing demand for sustainable transport in Europe and in new markets."
