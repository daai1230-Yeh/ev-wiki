# Germany: VWCV cuts prices for electric models by up to €10,000

**Author**: Sebastian Schaal | **Date**: 2026-04-21

---

Volkswagen Commercial Vehicles (VWCV) is offering a special discount of up to €10,000 on its electric models in Germany until 29 May 2026. Depending on the model, discounts range from €5,800 to €10,000 — available to both private and commercial customers.

The campaign targets vehicles registered for the first time by 31 December 2026.

**Discount breakdown:**
- VW ID. Buzz: up to €6,000 off (private customers)
- VW e-Caravelle: up to €6,700 off (private customers)
- Commercial customers receive higher discounts, with the top reduction of €10,000 applied to select commercial models

The move comes as VWCV looks to clear inventory and boost EV adoption amid softening demand for electric commercial vehicles in Germany. The campaign applies across several models in the VWCV electric lineup, running through the end of May 2026.
