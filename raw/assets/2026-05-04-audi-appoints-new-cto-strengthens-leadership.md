# AUDI appoints new Chief Technical Officer and strengthens leadership team

**Author**: Sebastian Schaal | **Date**: 2026-04-30

---

Audi has announced several coordinated personnel changes in key leadership positions, affecting both Audi AG and the SAIC-AUDI joint venture (Chinese sub-brand). Current CEO Fermín Soneira will move to Lamborghini from July 1, 2026.

## Key Changes

**Fermín Soneira → Lamborghini CTO (from July 1, 2026)**
Previously overseeing the SAIC-Audi collaboration and the AUDI sub-brand in China, Soneira moves to Italy as Chief Technical Officer at Automobili Lamborghini. He succeeds Rouven Mohr.

**Rouven Mohr → Audi AG Board Member for Technical Development (from March 1, 2026)**
The former Lamborghini CTO has been in dual role; the arrangement concludes in summer 2026.

**Fred Schulze → CEO of Audi-SAIC cooperation project (China)**
Currently Plant Manager in Neckarsulm; takes over Soneira's China role.

**Thomas Bogus → Plant Manager Neckarsulm**
Previously CEO of Audi Brussels (closed February 2025); takes over the Neckarsulm plant producing A6/A7 combustion and hybrid models.

## Strategic Context

- Neckarsulm plant to also develop AI expertise alongside luxury vehicle manufacturing
- The current Audi A8 flagship will be discontinued without a direct EV successor (though future return on an electric platform remains possible)
- Audi CEO Gernot Döllner made the appointments, emphasizing technical expertise and EV/AI focus
