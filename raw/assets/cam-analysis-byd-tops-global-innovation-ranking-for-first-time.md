# CAM analysis: BYD tops global innovation ranking for first time

**Author**: Florian Treiss | **Date**: 23.04.2026 - 13:00

---

The Center of Automotive Management (CAM) has, for the first time since the beginning of its rankings of the world's most innovative automakers, placed a Chinese manufacturer at the top: BYD. The top six positions are all occupied by Chinese and German manufacturers.

Unlike the 'CAM Electromobility Report,' which assessed the innovation strength of 36 car manufacturers in the battery-electric vehicle segment and identified Geely as the leader, the newly published 22nd edition of the 'Automotive Innovations' study series examines innovations across all drive types.

The analysis evaluated 867 innovations from the 2025/2026 period, including approximately 660 series innovations from 36 automotive groups. The data is compiled by the Center of Automotive Management (CAM) at the University of Applied Sciences.
