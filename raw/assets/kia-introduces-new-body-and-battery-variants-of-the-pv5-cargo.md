# Kia introduces new body and battery variants of the PV5 Cargo

**Author**: Sebastian Schaal | **Date**: 23.04.2026 - 12:00

---

Kia is expanding its model range with the PV5 Cargo high-roof variant (L2H2) and the shorter PV5 Cargo L1H1, with plans to offer the electric van in three-panel versions in the future. These variants will also introduce a new base battery option.

The two new variants of the PV5 Cargo made their European debut at the Commercial Vehicle Show 2026 in Birmingham this week (21 to 23 April). The new PV5 L2H2 is 4.67 metres long, the same as the familiar L2H1, but slightly taller at 2.21 metres compared to the previous 1.82 metres. The additional 30 centimetres in height directly increases the cargo volume significantly.
