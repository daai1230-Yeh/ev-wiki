# Olectra Greentech partners Dassault Systèmes to digitise EV development

**Author**: Auto industry press | **Date**: 11 May 2026

---

Olectra Greentech Limited, India's leading electric bus manufacturer, announced on May 11 that it will adopt Dassault Systèmes' 3DEXPERIENCE platform, marking a significant step toward building a globally benchmarked, digitally integrated product development ecosystem for electric mobility.

## Partnership Overview

Under the collaboration, Olectra will implement Dassault Systèmes' 3DEXPERIENCE platform to create a model-based and platform-led product development environment. The platform connects design, engineering, simulation and manufacturing teams through a unified digital framework, enabling real-time collaboration across the complete vehicle lifecycle.

The 3DEXPERIENCE platform connects people, data, design, simulation, and manufacturing within a unified, collaborative environment, enabling a real-time, end-to-end view of the entire product lifecycle. The platform will integrate product design, simulation, manufacturing, and lifecycle management into a single collaborative environment, enabling Olectra to transition toward a model-based engineering approach aligned with global OEM practices.

The approach is expected to help manufacturers reduce product development timelines, improve vehicle validation processes and accelerate innovation cycles. The platform will also enable virtual simulation and validation before physical prototyping, helping reduce costs and improve development efficiency.

## About Olectra

Olectra Greentech Limited, part of the MEIL Group, is India's largest manufacturer of pure electric buses and electric tippers, and a pioneer in the country's electric mobility ecosystem. With over 3,800 electric vehicles deployed nationwide, an order book exceeding 10,000 vehicles, and more than 550 million green kilometres clocked, Olectra combines indigenous engineering, operational reliability, and continuous innovation to drive India's transition to clean, efficient, and zero-emission transportation.

## Strategic Significance

The partnership between Olectra and Dassault Systèmes signals how India's EV industry is steadily moving towards globally aligned digital manufacturing and engineering standards, setting the stage for the next wave of electric commercial vehicle innovation.

By embedding the 3DEXPERIENCE platform at the core of its engineering ecosystem, and through close collaboration with Dassault Systèmes, Olectra aims to enhance its digital capabilities and set new benchmarks for how electric vehicles are conceptualized, engineered, and brought to market.
