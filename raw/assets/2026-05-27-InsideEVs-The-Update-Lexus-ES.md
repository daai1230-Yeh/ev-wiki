**2027 Lexus ES Review: Which Is Better, The Hybrid Or The EV?**
Mack Hogan drove both the hybrid and the electric versions of the 2027 Lexus ES in California. He found the EV far better than prior Lexus and Toyota EVs, marking a notable step up for the brand's electric efforts.

**Everyone Seems To Hate Ferrari's First EV. I Don't**
A defense of the Ferrari Luce, Ferrari's first battery-electric supercar, which drew widespread scorn over its Jony Ive–firm design. InsideEVs argues the car is misunderstood.

**This EV Battery Is Terrifyingly Over-Engineered**
The Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe's all-electric powertrain uses an extraordinarily comprehensive heat-management and cooling system to enable 600 kW peak charging.

**The U.S. Banned Chinese Car Tech—But Volvo Just Got A Special Pass To Keep It**
Volvo received U.S. authorization to import connected vehicles despite its Geely ownership ties, an exception to the broader ban on Chinese vehicle technology.
