# Polestar 3 gains 800V system and faster charging

**Author**: electrive editorial team | **Date**: 2026-04-27

---

Polestar is upgrading the Polestar 3 with an 800-volt architecture enabling up to 350 kW DC charging, along with 'Breathe Charge' software that optimises charging speed and battery health, cutting 10–80% charging time to 22 minutes.

## 800V Architecture

The upgrade involves extensive component changes including new batteries, motors, inverters, and cable harnesses. The architecture shift to 800V reduces energy losses, lowers thermal stress on components, and reduces cabling requirements. Peak DC charging rises from 250 kW to 350 kW — approximately 25% faster.

## Breathe Charge Software

Alongside the hardware upgrade, Polestar has integrated Breathe Charge, an adaptive battery management software developed by Breathe Battery Technologies. The system continuously monitors battery state in real time, adjusting charging speed on the fly to suit temperature, battery chemistry, and overall condition.

Key benefits:
- A 10-minute charging session with Breathe Charge delivers up to **38% more added range** compared with the same session without the software
- Enables comparable range additions in cold-weather conditions to those achieved in optimal temperatures without the software

## Performance Upgrades

The new in-house developed permanent magnet synchronous rear motor boosts output significantly:
- Single-motor: 333 hp (up from 295 hp)
- Dual-motor: 544 hp (up from 483 hp)
- Performance variant: up to 680 hp

## Computing Power

The 2026 Polestar 3 uses the Nvidia Drive AGX Orin chip, increasing computing power from 30 to 254 trillion operations per second (TOPS) — an eightfold increase benefiting active safety systems, battery performance, and sensor data processing. Existing owners will be offered a complimentary retrofit of the computer to the next-generation unit.
