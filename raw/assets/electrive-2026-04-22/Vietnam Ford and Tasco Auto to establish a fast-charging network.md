# Vietnam: Ford and Tasco Auto to establish a fast-charging network

**Author**: Sagar Parikh | **Date**: 22.04.2026 - 14:30

---

Ford Motor Company has joined hands with Tasco Auto, its local distributor, to develop a network of fast-charging stations in Vietnam. The duo signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) for the new initiative in Hanoi recently.

Ford and Tasco Auto together plan to set up more than 89 DC charging stations across Vietnam. The Blue Oval will invest in 34 DC charging stations, targeting completion by the end of the third quarter of 2026. The homegrown automotive distributor will invest in over 55 DC charging stations and expects to complete its expansion by the end of 2026. The company may source the chargers from Esky Corporation, with which it earlier signed an agreement to deploy over 50 DC charging stations starting in early 2026.

Currently, Ford has deployed 16 DC charging stations and 34 AC charging stations at its local dealerships and service centres across Vietnam, and is now working to expand its charging footprint significantly.
