What is TIK New Mobility

Transformation and Innovation Consortium, a consortium of stakeholders aiming
to transform the Slovak automotive & mobility sector.
The TIKs are a long-term strategic initiative supported by grant from Recovery and
Resilience Facility, covering their activities in 2023 - 2026.

Our Vision

TIK Mobility will be the driver for change in Slovak automotive & mobility sector:

supporting the shift in production value from ICE vehicle manufacturing to
electric vehicles, EV-related components, and battery production,
paving the way for adoption of smart and sustainable mobility solutions within
the frame of urban innovations, including connected and automated mobility
concepts.

Our objectives

TIK Mobility aims at:

fostering innovation ecosystem at national & international level,
generating new R&D&I projects and developing new products, 
transforming the high value-added production capacities, and attracting
new investment in innovative technologies,  
growing the talent and supporting creation of workplaces with high added
value.

Types of activities

joint activities of partners to develop and validate new innovations and
products,
facilitating use of public R&D infrastructure for project partners,
stimulating research projects, connecting researchers, startups, experts and
pilot projects (proof-of-concept),
supporting the acquisition of new talents and experts from abroad
(doctoral/postdoctoral scholarship),
Technology transfer from academic field to practice,
partnerships between private and academic fields, startups, research
institutions and large enterprises,
ecosystem activities (incubators, accelerators and support programs),
increasing the capacity of scientific and research institutions to cooperate
with industry.

Our planned results

improved performance and quality of battery technologies, including the
new generation of battery cells,
innovations relevant for electric vehicles such as for the propulsion
mechanism including the electric motor, transmission, power electronics
as well as sensors, energy management systems, software, and other types
of products
increased number of smart & sustainable urban innovations adopted
through the approach of city as a living lab.
increased human capital in the field of R&D&I
new public-private partnerships in the field of R&D&I,
increased number of new innovative businesses (startups) successful in
the national and international environment,

As TIK partner, you can:

pursue collaborations & synergies with partners, 
get your own grant to fund your R&D&I activities, 
get access to public R&D infrastructure,
benefit of services, such as technology transfer services, talent circulation etc.
