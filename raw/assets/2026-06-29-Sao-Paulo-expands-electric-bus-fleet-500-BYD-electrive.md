# São Paulo expands electric bus fleet with 500 new vehicles

**Author**: Carla Westerheide | **Date**: 2026-06-29

---

São Paulo introduced 500 new battery-electric buses, increasing its fleet to **1,759 ZEV buses** (inc. 189 trolleybuses) — Brazil's largest. BYD supplied **265 vehicles** (D9W + BC22 articulated with Blade Battery). City confirms **no more diesel-only bus procurement**.

Latin America's electric bus fleet now exceeds **10,000 vehicles**, with Chile, Colombia and Brazil accounting for 80% of deployments.
