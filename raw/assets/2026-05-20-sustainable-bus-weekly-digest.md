# Sustainable Bus Weekly — May 20, 2026

**Source**: Sustainable Bus Weekly (info@sustainable-bus.com)

---

## Highlights This Week

**Menarini unveils new e-bus range at Next Mobility Exhibition 2026 (Milan)**
World premiere of Menarini's new electric bus lineup at NME 2026. Menarini is one of Italy's historic bus manufacturers, now pushing into full electrification.

**Yutong, Solaris and Irizar at NME 2026**
All three major e-bus manufacturers showcased latest technologies and strategies at the Next Mobility Exhibition in Milan. Solaris presented the **Urbino 10.5 electric** for the Italian market.

**BVG (Berlin) launches tender for 106 electric buses**
Berlin's transit authority BVG launched a tender for subcontracted lines encompassing 106 electric buses.

**MAN secures order for 112 electric buses in Vilnius**
MAN Truck & Bus secured an order to supply 112 electric buses to the Lithuanian capital.

**Limoges (France) advances electrification with Iveco Bus**
French city of Limoges moves forward on fleet electrification plans in partnership with Iveco Bus.

## Sector Context

Around **105,000 bus and coach driver positions remain unfilled across Europe** (IRU figures), as public transport operators enter a period where staffing pressure and fleet electrification converge at the bus depot.
