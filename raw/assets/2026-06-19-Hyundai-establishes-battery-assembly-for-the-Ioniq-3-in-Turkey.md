# Hyundai establishes battery assembly for the Ioniq 3 in Turkey

**Author**: Daniel Bönnighausen | **Date**: 18.06.2026

---

Hyundai is expanding production of the Ioniq 3 at its Turkish plant in İzmit to include its own battery assembly. The new facility is part of a €715 million investment package.

Series production of the Ioniq 3 begins in August. The battery assembly investment totals €55 million of the €715m package, on a 30,000 m² site. In collaboration with Hyundai Mobis, assembly will use 27 robots, with 300+ new jobs created initially.

**Production targets**: 27,000 Ioniq 3 units in 2026, rising to 40,000+ in 2027. The battery line is also intended to support future EV and hybrid models.

This marks the first BEV production at İzmit (3.3 million vehicles built since 1997), making Hyundai the first foreign manufacturer of pure EVs in Turkey after domestic producer Togg.

**Ioniq 3 specs**: Based on E-GMP (400V). Two battery sizes: 42.2 kWh (Standard Range, up to 334 km WLTP) and 61 kWh (Long Range, up to 496 km WLTP). LFP cells (shorter range) sourced from China; NMC cells expected from Hungary.
