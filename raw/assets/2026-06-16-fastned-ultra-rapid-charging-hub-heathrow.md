# Fastned launches first 'ultra-rapid' charging hub near Heathrow

**Author**: Ciaran Daly | **Date**: 2026-06-15

---

Fastned and Places for London have opened their first ultra-fast EV charging hub at Hatton Cross near Heathrow Airport — the first of up to 25 hubs planned for the British capital.

The new site features twelve 400kW charging bays near the M25, M4, and A30 motorways. It includes extra-wide bays, two accessible charging spaces, solar canopies, and 24/7 access and security. To celebrate the opening, free charging is offered from 15–19 June.

The facility serves private motorists, taxis, and commercial fleets. Further planned sites include a flagship 36-bay location at Hanger Lane and an 8-bay hub at East Finchley Underground station, with several more in planning across Newham, Haringey, and Barking and Dagenham.

Deputy Mayor for Environment Mete Coban noted: "This is the first of 25 new ultra-rapid charging hubs planned across London, helping make charging easier and more convenient, particularly for people who don't have access to off-street parking." The Mayor has committed to supporting rollout of up to 40,000 EV charge points across London by 2030.
