# Moto to 'quadruple' number of EV charging bays

**Author**: Ciaran Daly | **Date**: 2026-04-30

---

Moto Hospitality, the UK's largest motorway services operator (60+ sites), has announced a fourfold increase in EV charging bays across its sites, plus 15 eHGV superhubs for electric freight.

## Expansion Plans

- **Passenger EVs**: Quadruple existing capacity (from 1,000+ ultra-rapid bays installed over the past 5 years)
- **eHGV Superhubs**: 15 large-scale sites, each with up to 100 charging bays for electric trucks, positioned along key freight corridors
- **Planning approvals already secured**: New sites in Swindon and a dedicated truck stop in Wrotham, Kent

## Investment

- **Total planned**: £500 million investment programme
- **Already invested**: £300 million over the past five years
- Secured long-term lease extensions at 10+ key service station sites, providing stability for the investment programme

CEO Ken McMeikan: "Securing long term lease extensions at 10 of our key sites allows us to unlock our planned £500 million investment programme."
