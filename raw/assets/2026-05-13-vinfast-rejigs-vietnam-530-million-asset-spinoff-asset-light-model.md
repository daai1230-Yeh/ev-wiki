# VinFast Rejigs Vietnam Business: $530M Asset Spinoff Targets Faster Profitability

**Author**: ETAuto / 綜合報導 | **Date**: May 13, 2026

---

Vietnamese EV maker VinFast Auto announced on May 12, 2026 a major restructuring of its Vietnamese operations, planning to sell its manufacturing subsidiary for approximately US$530 million (VND 13.3 trillion) to pursue an asset-light business model targeting profitability by 2027.

## The Restructuring Plan

Under the plan, VinFast will split certain assets from VinFast Trading and Production JSC (VFTP)—its Vietnamese manufacturing subsidiary—into a newly established entity called **VinFast Vietnam JSC (VFVN)**. VFVN will hold strategic "soft" assets including:

- Global R&D activities and intellectual property
- Sales and after-sales systems
- Subsidiaries in Vietnam, Australia, and Germany

The remaining manufacturing-focused VFTP will then be sold to a buyer group led by Future Investment Research and Development JSC for approximately US$530 million. VinFast will repay roughly US$404.8 million of P-Note debt from the proceeds.

## Asset-Light Strategy

VinFast stated the restructuring would allow the company to focus on higher value-added activities such as product development, technology, brand building, and global sales—while gaining greater flexibility to respond to evolving EV technologies and market dynamics.

Under a new cost-plus contract manufacturing deal, VinFast will continue using the Vietnamese factory for production, but without carrying the capital expenditure burden of ownership.

## Timeline

The restructuring is slated for completion by Q3 2026, pending shareholder and creditor approvals. Overseas plants (in North Carolina, India, and Indonesia) remain untouched by this restructuring.

## Context

VinFast has faced mounting losses and cash flow challenges since its 2023 NASDAQ listing. The asset-light pivot mirrors strategies used by other capital-constrained EV startups globally—separating manufacturing assets from IP and brand to free up capital for product development and market expansion.
