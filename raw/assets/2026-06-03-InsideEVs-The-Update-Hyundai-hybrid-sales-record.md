# InsideEVs: The Update — June 2, 2026

**Source**: InsideEVs (newsletter@update.insideevs.com)

---

**Hyundai And Kia's Hybrids Are Crushing It Amid The Gas Crisis**
With gas prices persistently high due to the war in Iran, Hyundai and Kia posted an all-time record for sales of electrified models (hybrids, plug-in hybrids, and EVs) last month. Models like the Hyundai Tucson and Santa Fe hybrids, and Kia siblings the Sportage and Sorento, saw tremendous growth as consumers shied away from costly gas.

**The Company That Builds The iPhone Just Unveiled A Tesla Model Y Competitor**
Foxconn's Cavira (built by its Foxtron unit) gets an LFP battery and a dual-motor powertrain delivering up to 468 horsepower — not an Apple Car, but a Model Y rival.

**The Rolls-Royce Spectre Gets Big Update. But You Wouldn't Know It**
The updated Spectre goes farther, charges faster, and responds more sharply, while its design remains mostly untouched.

**Tesla Is Selling Discounted Used Cybertrucks Now**
The cheapest used Cybertruck in Tesla's inventory is now under $67,000 — raising the question of whether that's enough to convince buyers.
