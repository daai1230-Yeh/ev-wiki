# VW's EV offensive in China takes shape

**Author**: Sebastian Schaal | **Date**: 22.04.2026 - 09:34

---

Ahead of the Auto China 2026, the Volkswagen Group took the wraps off four all-electric world premieres. However, not all of them will launch under the VW brand—and not all models feature exclusively VW technology.

The premieres include the ID. UNYX 09 saloon (developed jointly with Xpeng in just 24 months), the mid-range SUV ID. AURA T6 from FAW-Volkswagen, the JETTA X showcar for the entry-level segment, and the exterior design of the E7X, the second production vehicle from the China-exclusive brand AUDI. Additionally, the company presented the already known Audi A6L e-tron, the China-specific long-wheelbase version of the electric A6.

The vehicles and concept studies unveiled at the Group Media Night ahead of the motor show are part of what the German carmaker describes as its 'largest-ever electrification offensive in China.' More than 20 new electric models are planned for the Chinese market as VW accelerates its push to compete with local EV brands.
