# electrive COMMERCIAL Newsletter – June 18, 2026

**Newsletter**: electrive COMMERCIAL | **Date**: June 18, 2026

---

> "Electrification is increasingly shifting from a technology discussion to an infrastructure and business model discussion." — Carla Westerheide, Editor, electrive

## Featured: Buhck e-truck TCO After 4 Years

German environmental services provider Buhck reports ~15% lower costs vs diesel in 2025, ~€125/day savings per vehicle, with 500,000+ km and 550 tonnes CO₂e avoided. Key factor: access to low-cost depot charging. (See full article.)

## Electric Buses

**London reaches 3,000 zero-emission buses**: One-third of TfL's fleet now zero-emission. Saves 100,000+ tonnes of CO₂/year. Fleet expected to double in five years. (See full article.)

**Zenobē raises £980m**: UK fleet electrification specialist secures financing for 1,200+ additional electric buses and charging infrastructure across UK and Ireland.

**14 new BYD BD11 buses in Northumberland**: Funded with North East Combined Authority support, operating from Arriva's Blyth depot.

**VHH opens expanded e-bus depot in Norderstedt**: Hamburg-Holstein authority's expanded depot now has 49 charging points with space for 60 buses.

**SamTrans orders hydrogen bus hub in California**: Contract for hydrogen refuelling station for up to 175 fuel cell buses. First commercial use of Bosch Rexroth's CryoPump technology.

**Shillong deploys first electric buses**: Five EVs in service in Meghalaya, northeastern India — marking the city's entry into electric public transport.

## Electric Trucks

**Scania: 105-truck order from Wibax**: EU's largest electric truck order for bulk transport from Swedish logistics firm Wibax. Includes fleet optimisation services.

**China plan for heavy-duty EV trucks**: Government targets 40% NEV share for trucks over 12 tonnes by 2030, subsidising ~3,000 charging and battery swap stations.

**Einride goes public on Nasdaq**: Swedish electrified/autonomous freight provider Einride listed via SPAC merger. Stock temporarily gained 100%+ on first trading day.

**UltraTech Cement deploys 45 e-trucks in India**: Electric fleet now totals 89 units across India operations.

**Sany delivers 50 autonomous mining trucks**: Self-driving electric trucks with 90-tonne payloads and 801 kWh CATL batteries deployed in Inner Mongolia.

**New Energy Transport (Australia)**: 20 electric prime movers deploying after $5M funding raise, supported by six mobile ultra-fast charging units.

## Charging Infrastructure

**Weihenstephan e-truck charging depot**: Bavarian State Brewery combines high-power charging, battery storage, and solar at its Freising site.
