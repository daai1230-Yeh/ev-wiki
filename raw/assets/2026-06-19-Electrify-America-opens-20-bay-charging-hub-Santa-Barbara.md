# Electrify America opens 20-bay charging hub in Santa Barbara

**Author**: Carla Westerheide | **Date**: 18.06.2026

---

Electrify America has opened a new large-format charging station in downtown Santa Barbara, California — 20 charging bays (up to 350 kW) with an integrated 1.9 MW battery energy storage system (BESS), the largest public battery storage deployment in its network to date.

The BESS stores grid electricity during low-demand and high-renewable periods, then discharges during peak charging to support capacity, reduce grid impact, and maximise renewable use.

This is Electrify America's second site in Santa Barbara, joining large-format California hubs in Santa Monica, San Diego, and San Francisco. All 20 bays launch with CCS connectors; some will convert to NACS later this summer as part of an ongoing NACS pilot.

"By incorporating advanced storage technology, we're able to bring Hyper-Fast charging to locations that have traditionally been difficult to serve," — Rob Barrosa, President and CEO of Electrify America.
